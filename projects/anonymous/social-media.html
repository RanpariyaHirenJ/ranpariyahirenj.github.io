Social Media Marketing

Markets have become more demanding, competition is fierce and diverse, and products of all stripes have been forced to evolve and adapt to a rapidly shifting marketplace.
One area in particular has become a fierce battleground -- social media. We engage in industry benchmarking of brands to see how it stacks up in social media against direct competitors. We analyse and identify social media data and pitch the brands business pitch to create vanity metrics in order to create an impressive broader audience and social influencers in the social media platform.
