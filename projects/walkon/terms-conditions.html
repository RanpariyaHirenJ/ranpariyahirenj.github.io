Background

  • KALPATARU will conduct a corporate wellness program accessible through its online platform known as www.walk-on.co.in ("the Website") and GOQii will be the fitness partner ("GOQii") wherein the employees of the Participating Company (Kalpataru Limited and Argos International) will take part in a race around the 'virtual world', scheduled for the period starting from 7th November 2016 to 6th February 2017 ("the Event Period"), in teams of 5 (five) Walk-Onites(each a "Team"), by recording daily step counts ("the Event");
  • The Participating Company wishes to enrol one or more Teams to take part in the Event;
  • This document sets out the terms and conditions governing the conduct of the Event by Walk-Onitesand the participation by the Participating Company and its employees in the Event.

There will be two registration forms (Individual and Group Registration form) enabling an individual or a group of five individuals to register as a team.
  • Walking Guru: Each and every participant and team will be represented by one of the Walking Gurus. The Participating Employee will, at the time of completing the Registration Form, select a Walking Guru. The Participating Employees agree that for the purpose of the Event and all matters related thereto, Team Walk-On shall & respective HR representatives shall interact and coordinate with them. There will be no direct interaction, of any nature whatsoever, between the Walk-Oniteand any member of Team Walk-On as provided on the website or on a mutually agreeable basis.

  • Login ID: The login ID will be the email address used to register for the event in the Registration Form.
  • Walk Pack: Upon confirmed registration, Team Walk-On will dispatch the Walk Packs to the Participating Employees. Every Walk-On participant in the event shall be entitled to a Walk Pack containing only a GOQii Fitness Band with Inbuilt USB type Charger.
  • Delivery of Walk Pack: A Walk Pack for each confirmed registered employee of the Participating Company will be delivered to their location as mentioned during the registration process. Walk Packs will only be delivered to confirmed and registered participants only.
