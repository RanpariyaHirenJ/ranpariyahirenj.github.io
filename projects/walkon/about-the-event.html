Walk-on 2016

Walk-on 2016 is a unique, fun and fitness band based event, powered by GOQii, that will run for 90 consecutive days. The event is designed to motivate participants regardless of gender, region or age to take a minimum of 10,000 steps a day, as recommended by global health authorities.Also every participant is assigned a Coach, as per the health goals, backed by experts who will assist them in achieving their health goals. The Coaches will guide, motivate, monitor eating habits and have an interaction with participants over chat or voice call.

The idea is to promote an active lifestyle and teamwork and create a happier, healthier and more productive workforce as well as a 'culture of health' within the company. The event is open to employees of Kalpataru Limited's Real Estate Division and Argos International.

How does it work?

All employees will be informed to register for participation on the Walk-on website.

There are five groups under which teams will be formed. These groups are represented by five 'Walking Gurus'. Each participant will be allocated to a team under his/her chosen 'Walking Guru'. Participants have the choice of forming their own teams of five under the "Walking Guru" of their Choice or register as an individual.

As soon as the employee registers he/she will be provided the Walk-On + GOQii Fitness Band Kit.

The kit is a GOQii branded box inside which there is a fitness band with inbuilt charger and a unique activation code.

The participant post confirmation of his/her participation will Download, from respective stores, the GOQii app on their Android/iOS devices and punch in the unique reference id (inside the GOQii Box).

The fitness band will have to be synced with the app downloaded on phone via Bluetooth.

The app will ask personal health related questions in order to ascertain the health goal of the participant.

The pedometer counts the number of steps taken daily and evaluates the employee's Fitness Quotient (number of steps).

Every participant is required to wear the fitness band from the start of the event.

The fitness band keeps a track of the activity/steps of the participant. At the end of the day the participant should sync the fitness band with the phone via Bluetooth to update their fitness quotient (step count).

The score updated will be reflected on the website for the teams to see and compete with other teams.

At the end of exciting 90 days, the team with the highest accumulated steps will be declared the winner and awarded. Team Walk-On will also have rewards for best performing individuals/teams at various milestones.

The websitewill provide statistics like the average step count for a period, the leader board, health tips, exercise tips, etc.
