Home/ Privacy Policy

Kalpataru Walk-on 2016 powered by GOQii is an in-house event and this means that only employees of the Kalpataru Limited and Argos International are allowed to participate in it.We are committed to protecting your privacy while you compete in this fun event. Our policy allows you to choose and control the use of the nature and extent of the information you provide us.

Collection of information
During the course of the event, you will be asked to provide us with certain information that is essentially collected to make Kalpataru Walk-On more rewarding for you. We will collect personal information like your name, email address, employee ID/postal address which will be required for your identification and participation in this event. Sometimes, we may specifically ask you for personal information when you sign-up through this website. This information may be used to keep you updated about the contest. We may also ask you about your interests and hobbies but it isn't mandatory to provide us with this information.

Use of anonymous information
Walk-On automatically collects anonymous information to monitor the access and use of this website. We may use or disclose such anonymous, non-personally identifiable aggregate data to any third party or management at our sole discretion.

Disclosure
Besides the exceptions mentioned on this page, the rest of all your personal information will remain undisclosed.

Personal informations
If we do hold your personal information, we will, wherever possible, provide you with access to it. We may use this data for internal purposes without prior consent.
