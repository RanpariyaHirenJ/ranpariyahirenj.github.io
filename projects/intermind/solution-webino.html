Webino

Webino is electrical appliance automation, surveillance and access control solution based on the Arduino open source embedded platform.

It comprises of an electrical appliance controller for controlling the lights and appliances of your house or property, a motion sensor camera for monitoring unauthorized intrusion and a RFID tag reader to login / logout access requests.

It can be operated using smart phone based mobile app over Wi-Fi from within the house and over the internet for remote operations and surveillance.

Added features like the electricity consumption report and appliance usage matrix helps one keep a check on the power consumption levels of their property, presence detection log keeps a photographic track of the unauthorized presence and access log help identify reporting times as well as intrusion detection.

Webino Highlights

  • Can be easily retrofitted into an existing infrastructure so no need of any extra wiring.
  • Operates on WiFi over PSA PSK2 secure network.
  • Can be accessed and operated using a very user-friendly mobile app and website.
  • Graphical reports can be generated to keep a check on power consumption and appliance usage.
  • Photographic intrusion log can be generated with time stamp to identify and track unauthorised presence.
  • Access log with in time / out time information help us keep a check on reporting and unauthorized access.
  • Affordable solution with a 1 year replacement warranty on hardware.
