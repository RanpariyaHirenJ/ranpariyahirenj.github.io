Digital Ammunition for COVID Warriors

Intermind salutes the COVID warriors, be it our policemen, doctors, delivery boys, medical equipment manufacturers, farmers, drivers and volunteers playing their part in making it a bit easier for us to face this pandemic.

We have been working day and night, enabling these warriors with the right digital tools to ensure that they can offer their services to their full potential. We provide these services at cost and at a 20% faster turnaround time.

e-Commerce

  • From experience, we already know that a standard e-Commerce offering does not work in these testing times. Courier services are not able to honour delivery timelines due to unpredictable logistics, returns are even more difficult to manage, predicting order volumes is best left to the higher powers and keeping your employees and contract workers safe becomes your primary priority.
  • Our e-commerce solutions address all these issues. Instead of trying to reinvent the wheel, just give us a call and our experts will guide you on how to implement online ordering for your business.

Skill Assessment

  • Intermind's enterprise skill assessment SaaS solution Assessmo is available of the shelf for sunrise industries that are hiring virtually. Assessmo's Artificial Intelligence algorithms access the vast bank of available MCQs for various skill sets and evaluate candidates in real time. Based on the candidate's response, the algorithm chooses the next question. Assessmo's evaluation is thorough and scientific and is even better than face to face evaluation.
  • Assessmo can also be used to independently evaluate the effectiveness of your online training programs. It can provide you with reports on how effective each training program is. If you provide certificates after successful completion of training, Assessmo can automate that process too.

Video Conferencing

  • After zooming in and out of various conferencing softwares, if you are worried about security, not able to fathom the myriad features they offer or have no idea of the monthly bills that can hit you, we can help. We have created video conferencing solutions for online OPDs being used by almost 2000 doctors consulting 50,000 patients, an online learning marketplace and a B2B training institute.
  • We can integrate the portal we develop with video conferencing APIs from Microsoft & Google or we can custom develop your very own solution, where recurring costs will be low. We generate reports on the duration of each call, integrate files shared with a DMS of your choice (or just save it to the cloud) and can even automate billing.

Virtual Walkthroughs

  • Houses needs to be seen before buyers can make a purchase decision & new recruits need to be trained on machinery before they can hit the shop floor post lockdown. Our mixed reality product VR-Magic is a powerful walkthrough generator with a backend of its own.
