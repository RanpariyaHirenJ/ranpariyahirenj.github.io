About the Founder

Mr. Sagar Nikam was awarded 'Young Icon Of Nasik' by a website 'Right Step Ahead'. Also many write-ups are published in news papers appreciating his achievements. One of them is 'Mr. Sagar Nikam – A Multidimensional Personality published in "The Day View"

Mr. Sagar Nikam is the founder of ELC, who is an Alumnus of Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management, Mumbai (JBIMS). He was the MBA CET topper in 2008 and while pursuing his MBA full time course, he decided to start an institute of his own. Mr. Sagar Nikam has been a Topper throughout his career.
