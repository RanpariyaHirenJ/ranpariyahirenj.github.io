About Excellence Learning Centre Pvt. Ltd.

Excellence Learning Centre Pvt. Ltd. is an educational institute for MBA Aspirants. We guide the students for MBA Entrance Exams, namely MBA CAT, CMAT, SNAP, NMAT and others. ELC signifies 'The Quest for Excellence' the will to win, the desire to succeed, the urge to reach your full potential'... where you can get the creative spirit to unlock the doors to Excellence. The excellence of any coaching lies in the Appropriateness of Methodology which is our Uniqueness. Keeping this thought in mind, the founder of ELC, Mr. Sagar Nikam started this institute in 2010 focusing on his Unique Teaching Methodology. ELC's main focus areas are our Unique Speed Techniques and Individual Attention towards the students.

Our main objective has always been to impart quality in what we have to offer to our customer. We know what it takes to get success. It is our resolution to guide the beginners to reach their destination. We are here to show you the path of success on which we have traveled to achieve the top ranks.

Our main area is the MBA Entrance Exams, Campus Recruitment Training to help the students excel in the competitive and corporate fields respectively.

Journey of ELC

It all started with weekend batches, when Mr. Sagar was completing his MBA course in JBIMS. Being an iconoclast, he challenged the old tradition of chasing placements. While doing his summer internship, he was offered a Job from a MNC company, however he was determined to start his own entrepreneurship venture. He resigned from placements and instead started his first branch in Nashik, in a small premises of a shop…..and the maestro embarked upon his journey to achieve milestones!!! This also set a TREND in his college inviting more candidates to follow their vibrant dreams.

The Institute

We are one of the leading MBA entrance coaching institutes. The head office of Excellence Learning Centre Pvt Ltd is in Nashik. We have our operation in Pune, Mumbai, Aurangabad, Jalgaon and Nagpur. Excellence Learning Centre provides coaching for Students preparing for MBA entrance exams, Campus Placements and Bank PO.

Our Centers
