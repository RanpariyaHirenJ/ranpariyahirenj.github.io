PROGRAM FEES

1. Read carefully before proceeding

Sign-up & Register. Then pay fees individually or as a group.

  • Sign up and Register. It's free!
  • Sign-in & visit Pricing tab
  • Select Nationality (For individual or for the group, as the case may be) Please note that Nationality once entered cannot be changed
  • Registered individual is automatically the Primary Contact for the group. All communication will be with his/her Email address
  • Registered individual can Add Participants or Companions
  • Group members could be: Participants (who take part in training and optional paid events) or Companions ( who accompanies the participant but takes part ONLY in optional paid events)
  • Note: No Part/Full Cancellations or Refunds for non-participation or No show by participant/s or companion/s. Refunds will be made online in the unforeseen event of the program being cancelled by Aikido-India

2. Select Nationality

(For individual or for the group, as the case may be. One nationality applies for the group and must be supported with valid Government Issued identity Proof (Passport for Non-Indian nationals; Passport/Aadhar Card/Voter ID /PAN Card for Indian Nationals) for all in the group -- Uploaded and Verified with original at the venue. Please note that Nationality once entered cannot be changed. Program pricing is based on Nationality. If your group has mixed Indian/Non-Indian nationals write to us with details at aws.registration@aikido-india.com
