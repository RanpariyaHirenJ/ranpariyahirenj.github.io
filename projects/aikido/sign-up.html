Read Carefully

  • Registration is free
  • Signup / Signin to pay fees individually or as a group
  • Signin to visit the pricing tab
  • After registration, you will receieve an email at your specified email address to confirm your identity. kindly check your spam folder if you don't find it in your inbox
  • After signing in you will need to select your nationality before registering as a participant. Note that Nationality once selected cannot be changed
  • Registered individual is automatically the primary contact for the group. All communications will be with his or her email address
  • Registered user's can add participants or companions
  • Group members can be Participants (who take part in training and optional paid events) and Companions (Who accompanies the participants but takes part ONLY in optional paid events)
  • Note : No Part/Full Cancellations or Refunds for non-participation or No show by participant's or companion/s. Refunds will be made online in the unforeseen event of the program being cancelled by Aikido-India
