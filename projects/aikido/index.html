Aikido World Summit

Varanasi 7 – 9 February 2020

Block THE date

For the very first time in India, a world level meet of masters, enthusiasts, students and practitioners of the beautiful artistic discipline of Aikido. Tracing the lineage of martial arts originating from India and exploring the path of mythology, religion and cultural practices. A 3-day exhibition of mesmerizing skill, graceful displays, intense trainings and bonding at every level. For those who love Aikido, this is a never-to-miss event.

Sensei Mitsuteru Ueshiba - Dojo Cho, Aikikai, Hombu Dojo Japan, has most generously agreed to be present himself representing the Foundation and be the Lead Instructor for the program and seminars in Varanasi. He will be accompanied and supported by Makoto Ito Shihan who will conduct part of the training seminars.

Official representatives of the highest level will attend. Luminaries from all around the world and regional bodies of India will be present. Be in the company of masters and high practitioners.

At Varanasi. One of the oldest living cities in the world

Varanasi is the fountainhead of two major religions – Hinduism and Buddhism. Rich in Archaeological splendour, culture and heritage, Varanasi hails back to Vedic times. Believed to be founded by Lord Shiva according to Hindu mythology, Varanasi today is also a major tourist centre with carefully planned modern amenities while retaining many of the ancient traditions and practices. It will be enchanting to be hosted in a city lending special meaning to Aikido.

Getting around in Varanasi

Where to stay

Accommodation during the trip will be borne by the participant. Varanasi is one of the major tourist cities of India with a big share of foreign and domestic visitors. There are hotels, guest houses, homestays and lodges at different price points from the uber luxury to budget. Some hotels do offer airport transfer as part of their paid services or even include them in room tariff. Popular online portals offer a variety of options and discounts. Try booking.com, airbnb.com makemytrip.com, cleartrip.com, yatra.com, agoda.com, oyorooms.com.

To make your commute easy, you can consider staying in the Durgakund / Bhelupur area. The shuttle bus will operate from here on all three days. Some popular hotels here are: Hotel Diamond, Hotel Broadway and JS Residency

Airport Transfer

Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport is the international airport (25 KMs from Bhelupur). Prepaid taxis from airport and App based taxi services Uber and OLA are available. Our event partner Balaji Events & Production (contact numbers below) can assist you for hotel bookings, airport transfers and local sightseeing if required.
