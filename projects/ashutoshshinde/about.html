About

Ashutosh, a born nature lover, decided to pursue photography as a hobby after his visit to Gir National Park, Gujarat, India in 2013. Soon, Wildlife Photography once taken up as a hobby, turned into serious passion. His continual process of learning intricacies of photography and support from his mentors at DCP Expeditions LLP, helped him take this passion to the next level.

He has travelled across the different terrains of India & Kenya in search of biodiversity and its documentation in terms of Photographs. He has been successful in showcasing his knowledge in varied genre of Wildlife Photography. His work has been recognised by leading international nature and conservation institutions like NGC, BBC Wildlife Magazine, iNaturalist.org etc.

A banker by profession, Ashutosh, is a Photographer by Passion

He is currently associated with DCP Expeditions, India's one of the fastest growing Photography Training Academy, as Panel Expert and Consultant - Strategy Team"

This website is to showcase the work done by Ashutosh, in the field of Wildlife Photography and connects with enthusiast nature lovers and photographers across the globe.

For Ashutosh, photography is freezing a moment. A moment which is unique in its own, it is a memory which you wish to cherish for your life time. Photographing such beautiful moments is nothing but freezing those moments for lifetime. Photography is living life one more time.

Please feel free to contact Ashutosh, for Fine Art Print of the images, Wildlife Photography expeditions to Indian and international locations and photography workshops.
