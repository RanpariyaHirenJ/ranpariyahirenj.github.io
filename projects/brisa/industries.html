Sensors & Actuators

The key focus of IoT is data and it is crucial that this data is accurate. At Brisa, we are experts in Sensors and Actuators development which ensure that accuracy. We create a smart connect between people, machines and processes for unified functioning. With IoT, we take the evolutions of sensors and actuators to a completely different level.

Advantages of Brisa Sensors & Actuators

  • We combine multiple discipline capabilities and deliver technologically advanced sensor and actuator solutions from concept to prototyping, testing and full production.
  • We develop innovative, lower powered and wireless sensors to integrate into IoT system and solutions, while our actuators are the perfect solution for simple, safe and clean movement with accurate and smooth motion control.
  • Our sensors and sensor systems, which are produced via qualified industrial processes result in increased accuracy, decreased size and cost, and the ability to measure or detect things that weren't previously possible.
  • Our flexibility and dedication to the client ensure a superior custom-made and error-free product within a limited time frame.

Data Logging
