We are Brisa.

Brisa Technologies is a premier IT company offering a wide range of new age software products, services and solutions to the Automotive, Consumer Electronics, Domotics, Agriculture, Meteorology, Media and Semiconductor industries.

We offer exceptional value to our customers through our exceedingly competent interdisciplinary engagements and dedicated delivery teams who are at the forefront of these technically challenging areas. While our core business practices aim at providing industry leading solutions and services to our customers, we strive to keep ourselves on the cutting edge of technology by nurturing a research and development culture in the organization.
