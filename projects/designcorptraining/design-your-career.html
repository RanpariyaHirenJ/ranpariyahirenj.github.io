Design your career – For Graduating Students, specifically MBA Students

Yogesh had been highly successful in making a career in corporate life. He has worked in various multinational companies for the period of 17 years. For the purpose to bring positive change in students, he has started his move towards training students. He has authored quite a few books & created numerous Training DVDs.

DesignCorp is the Institute in the country to provide career development program for students in order to accomplish smooth landing in the corporate life from student life. This program improves the employability of students. It helps students to remain ahead among the peers when they join corporate. It is not just a series of lectures but it imparts hands on experience with the help of practical assignments.

This course would cover Presentation skills, Verbal and written Communication skills, Decision Making skills, Negotiation Skills, Interview skills, Make or Break Habits, Customer Management, Work life balance, Time Management, Thinking process, Positioning, Delegation, Goals, Implementation of ideas.

This course would increase confidence of students, It would make them able to work in Team, It would prepare them to become to deliver their peak performance in corporate. Improve decision making, Make them assertive.
Students would be evaluated individually as well as at their team level, on a scale of 1 to 7 for each assignment. This program would equip students to differentiate themselves compared to peers, during campus placements and other interviews. It would also provide techniques to prepare impactful CV.
