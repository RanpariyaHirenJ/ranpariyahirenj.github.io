Legislation Interpretation

INTERPRETIVE SERVICES

We render interpretive services interpreting legislation more broadly, understanding and the application of legislation to the issues and have delivered advice on several sensitive policy and regulatory issues.

We provide policy-based advice on corporate commercial issues, trade related issues, employment law issues, sector specific issues including superannuation industry issues.

We assist in the area of:

Migration

We provide assistance, advice and support in relation to:

  • various issues and requirements under the migration legislation including obtaining of all the relevant visas to enter Australia and overseas.
  • appearing before the Migration Review Tribunal in case on unfavourable decision from the Department of Immigration
  • liaising, interacting, dealing and appearing before the relevant authorities and bodies
  • determining and accessing the relevant categories of visa and then assist in making an application before the authorities for the Australian visa including the skills assessment.

On making a decision to arriving in Australia we can also assist you in identifying the right suburb, locations and schools and getting you connected with the community in Australia.

Property Laws

We provide assistance on varied issues associated with buying properties right from reviewing the contract through to the settlement stage providing advice on several issues associated with finance and purchase.

Financial Ombudsman, telecommunication Ombudsman and Review Tribunals

We provide assistance in resolving the disputes via lodging formal complaints against the businesses. We also assist in appearing before the relevant ombudsman and review tribunals. We have extensive knowledge and understanding of the telecommunication ombudsman and the financial services ombudsman and have appeared before VCAT for various consumer and trade law disputes and matters.

Building and Constructions Industry Disputes

We provide assistance in resolving the building and construction industry disputes and provide advice on the building industry legislations, issues associated with the building and development contract and the builders. We assist in mediation and effective dispute resolution including negotiations with the disputants in reaching resolution.

Research Projects

We do undertake research work on market sector or sector specific projects including education sector.
