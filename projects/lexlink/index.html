What we offer

Lex Link Offers end to end, one-stop services and dedicated legal solutions to the issues arising under Indian and Australian legal system. We assist Indians and Australians looking to set up, invest or venture in India. We provide advisory services in the areas of international trade, investments, exports import issues, licenses, licensing arrangements, resolution of dispute, dealing with relevant authorities and Government authorities, bodies both in India and Australia.
