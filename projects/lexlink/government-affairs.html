Government Affairs

Regulation And Compliance

We assist entities, residents, companies, in managing regulatory affair and compliance with various regulations and regulatory authorities' guidelines in both the jurisdictions.

We assist, advise, liaise, negotiate and deal with:

  • councils, other Government Bodies and Regulatory authorities
  • building industry issues, building commission, and building practitioner Boards,
  • Foreign Investment promotion boards, Department of Human Services, Centre Link, Department of Immigration and Multicultural Affairs, Foreign Trade Affairs

We Assit In Appearance

We assist and represent entities in the regulatory proceedings before various authorities on issues and matters related to merger control, abuse of dominance ad anti-competitive behaviour, tariff determination, licensing, regulation and adjudicatory proceedings.

We assist and represent entities before diverse regulators and judicial bodies such as:

  • the Electricity Regulatory Commissions,
  • the Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI),
  • the Competition Commission of India,
  • the Competition Appellate Tribunal, the Appellate Tribunal for Electricity (APTEL), arbitral tribunals including the courts in India.
