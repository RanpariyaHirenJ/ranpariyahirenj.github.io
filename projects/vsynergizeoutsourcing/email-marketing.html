FAQ - Email Marketing

What is Email Marketing?

Email Marketing is the act of sending a commercial message, typically to a group of people, using email. In its most general sense, every email sent to a potential or current customer could be considered Email Marketing. It usually involves using email to send advertisements, request business, or request sales or donations and is meant to build loyalty, trust, or brand awareness.

What are the benefits of Email Marketing?

Marketing your products or services by email is a fast, flexible and cost-effective way of reaching new customers and retaining existing customers by encouraging repeat website visits. Email marketing can also allow you to create targeted and personalized messages. This can help you to build meaningful relationships with your customers.

What is Email Marketing Automation?

Email Marketing Automation is a way for creating timely, relevant, personalized emails for individual contacts to be sent when they take a specific action. Email Marketing Automation isn't just for big companies. It helps you to collect leads, stay connected and connect customers automatically.

What are trigger emails?

Trigger emails are delivered to the customers' inboxes as a result of their behaviors. Most trigger-based emails are divided into two categories: Event and Segment. In event-based emails, messages triggered by actions performed by customers inside or outside your application that occur at a specific time.

What are operational emails?

An operational email is a way to communicate something that is crucial for customers either past or current to comprehend. Another major difference for operational emails is that these messages can be delivered to customers who have opted out of marketing communications from you.
