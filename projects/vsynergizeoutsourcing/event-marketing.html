FAQ - Event Marketing

How does your sales and marketing team follow up with trade show leads?

Trade shows are one of the most expensive tactics of lead generation where you incur the maximum Cost-per-Lead(CPL) and hence it is very important that each lead generated from the Trade show is followed up well on. The 1st email can start with a "Thank you for visiting Booth" along with a "Schedule for a Demo" or Phone call CTA. The responses can be then further divided into Nurture, Marketing Qualified Leads or Sales Qualified Leads.

What is Event Marketing Strategy?

Event Marketing is a promotional strategy that includes face-to-face communication between brands and their customers at events like conferences, trade shows, and seminars. Each event is different, it has different audiences, different content and different culture. Therefore, it deserves to be marketed in its own unique way.

How do you organize a Marketing Event?

When should you plan to start Event Marketing?

Promoting your event at least 90-180 days in advance is a great option. You need to analyze what's most suitable for your event. One important thing to do is to build your advertising and event marketing campaign from the time of announcement.
