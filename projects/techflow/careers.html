Tekla Modeler

Locations - Navi Mumbai, Jalgoan, Goa, Nasik, Ratnagiri, Kolhapur

Designation - Tekla Modeler

Education - B.E. /Diploma/I.T.I. in Civil / Mechanical (Preferred with AutoCad, Tekla, SDS/2 advance training course)

Experience -

  • Min. 3 years experience of Tekla editing and having good knowledge of detailing.
  • Min. 4 years modeling experience.

Professional Qualities -

  • Delivery of 100% accuracy in job.
  • Logical and practical thinking towards assigned job.
  • Quick action as per the client comment.

Technology Skill set -

  • Expertise in detailing of structural steel & Misc. steel
  • Sound knowledge of AISC, NISD, IBC and CISC standards of structural steel detailing.
  • Generating Pre-modeling RFI's and submitting it to Team Leader prior to start of Modeling.
  • Submitting post modeling RFI's before submitting model for checking.
  • Strictly maintaining modeling standards and protocols.
  • Modeling Steel in such a way that satisfies below noted three important points :-
    • Erection needs
    • Fabrication needs
    • Shipping needs
  • Giving idea about approximate time required for the Modeling to Team Leader while start of the allocated task.
  • Cross checking each and every connection and member placing done with contract document for the given task before submitting it for checking.
  • Attending all the comments received from checker or client, if not agreed on any comments, conveying it to Team Leader / Checker.
  • Setting the Prefix for the different Members and doing Numbering in the model.
  • Extracting Drawing as per sample to reduce amount of time required for detailing work.
  • Finalizing all settings related to Numbering for IFA, IFF & Revised submittals.
  • Keeping track of Numbering and submitted areas in the model.
  • Setting the prefix, suffix, member color and model status for the different members as per Customer needs.
  • Preparing & raising Job startup questions and RFI/Q&A to Customer.
Project Managers

Engineering Degree holders applying for the position of Project Managers need to have minimum of 4-5 years of experience on structural detailing including modeling and checking. He/She can be either Civil or Mechanical Engineer from a reputed engineering Institution.

Diploma Engineers applying for the position of Project Managers need to have minimum of 5-6 years of experience on structural detailing including modeling and checking. He/She can be either Civil or Mechanical Engineer from a reputed engineering Institution.

ITI Engineers or Draughtsman applying for the position of Project Managers need to have minimum of 7 years of work experience on structural detailing including modeling and checking. He/She can be either Civil or Mechanical Draughtsman from ITI.
He/She should be skilled enough to manage the entire project with a team of detailers, schedule staff and execute project precisely. Should have leadership qualities, management qualities and good communication skill in English as clients are based outside India. Should be able to lead and motivate team members for optimal production. Should have proficiency in IS or AISC standard. Should be deadline driven individual capable of multitasking.

Team Leaders

BE or Diploma Engineers (Civil/Mechanical) applying for the position of Team Leader needs to have minimum of 3-4 years experience in modeling and detailing and 1 year experience in checking.

ITI Draughtsman (Civil/Mechanical) applying for the position of Checkers needs to have minimum of 4-5 years experience in modeling and detailing and 2 years experience in checking.
He/She should have good knowledge in Tekla Structure software. Must to able to guide and facilitate team. Should have good written and verbal communication skill. Must be self motivated and multitasking individual.

Checkers

BE or Diploma Engineers (Civil/Mechanical) applying for the position of Checkers needs to have minimum of 3-4 years experience in modeling and detailing and 1 year experience in checking.

ITI Draughtsman (Civil/Mechanical) applying for the position of Checkers needs to have minimum of 4-5 years experience in modeling and detailing and 2 years experience in checking.
He/She should have high quality standard of working.

Modelers

BE or Diploma Engineers (Civil/Mechanical) applying for the position of Modelers needs to have minimum of 3-4 years experience in modeling (including detailing).

ITI Draughtsman (Civil/Mechanical) applying for the position of Modelers needs to have minimum of 4-5 years experience in modeling (including detailing). He/She should be experienced in modeling of stairs, ladders, handrails. Should be able to do Extraction of drawings as per client requirement, Numbering (Assign numbering etc). and Batch files settings.

Editor / Detailers

BE or Diploma Engineers (Civil/Mechanical) applying for the position of Editor / Detailers needs to have minimum of 6 months to 1 year experience in structural detailing on AutoCAD

ITI Draughtsman (Civil/Mechanical) applying for the position of Editor / Detailers needs to have minimum of 1 year experience in structural detailing on AutoCAD He/She should be experienced in Detailing extraction and detailing out shop drawings (beam, column etc), Preparing GA Drawings and Multi drawings.

Trainee Detailer

BE / Diploma Engineers / ITI Draughtsman (Civil/Mechanical) freshers can apply for the position of Trainee Detailer.
