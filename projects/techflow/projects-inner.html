The Capital C-70 | Seattle, USA

An exclusive architectural and close to nature splendor blend, has been depicted in one of a prestigious project of our client in Aarvali Resort, Goa project. This necklace shaped structure Resort consisting of steel is detailed by Techflow Engineers(I) Pvt Ltd.. Our scope included structural steel detailing of the roof of this piece of Art, With distinctive and tricky features like twisted members, each member having its own geometry with out absolutely no similarity with each other.

This structure was not only an intricate one but also needed a very tight schedule for delivery. Our team did a magnificent job of detailing on the steel of this project without any blunder and hassle for our client M/s. Shapoorji Pallonji.
