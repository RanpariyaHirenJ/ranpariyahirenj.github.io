Techflow is the world leading service offering company in structural engineering.

Specializing in the field of civil and structural engineering, Our Engineering team consists of qualified engineers and experienced designers.

Our Projects involve civil and structural work in Steel, Cement and process industries, Acid plants, copper smelters, zinc smelter etc. Technological structures for the process industries. Material handling systems such conveyors and conveyor galleries.

Projects are carried out overseas clients for Europe, Middle East, Africa & India.

We have complete expertise in structural segment right from design Analysis to detail engineering.
