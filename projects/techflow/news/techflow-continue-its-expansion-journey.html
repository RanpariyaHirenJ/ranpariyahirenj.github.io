Techflow continue its expansion journey

A new office space inaugurated in Mumbai for QC division Mumbai, 21 September 2017.

Techflow Engineers , the global leader in structural steel engineering services has expanded their operation further to create a dedicated QC team. Techflow's commitment to quality standards have resulted in client's absolute trust. Clients throughout the world have believed and entrusted with their most challenging and complex structures for error free project execution. Techflow has always accepted the challenge and has "stood up" to the client's expectation which helped them to strengthen its business relationship with its customers. To continue this quality deliverable approach , Techflow has invested heavily for their quality control division and now with new dedicated office for this QC division will not only help them to improve the client delivery but also make sure client get most needed value addition from the services offered by Techflow.

Commenting on this new office opening, Mr Uday Khandekar, MD of Techflow , who is presently traveling to Canada for their further international expansion said " We believe in sustainable growth, our commitment to our customer is very important aspect of our business. This new office for our dedicate QC team is step further towards our quality assurance commitment to our global customer community. We will continue to invest to make sure we continue to add value to our customer's project".

This new office at Rupa Solitaire building, MBP area of Navi Mumbai , is equipped with modern infrastructure, dedicate high end lease line to connect to all Techflow domestic and international locations. It has a capacity to accommodate 100 QC engineers and most importantly its close proximity with their corporate office will make it more efficient.

On this opening ceremony of new office, more than 150 Techflow Mumbai staffs and invited guests were present. Other distance location office staffs witnessed the ceremony through via live streaming.

This new office opening on the auspicious beginning of Navratri festival coincide the enthusiasm among all Tecflow team members, dressed in traditional outfits , celebrated the proud moments with whole hearted and spirit. Mr Arif Qureshi, VP operation & Priyanka Bagul , Head of QC congratulated whole Techflow team.
