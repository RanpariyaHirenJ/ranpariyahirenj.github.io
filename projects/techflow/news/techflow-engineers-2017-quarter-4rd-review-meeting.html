Techflow Engineers 2017 Quarter 4rd Review meeting

Techflow Engineers 2017 Q4 review meeting this time had a bigger perspective & purpose. 09th to 11th March 2018 at Sayaji Hotel, Kolhapur.

We involved more team members from different departments this time comparative to the last quarter review meeting, total 46 key employees were part of this review meeting.

Apart from the management, HR and Technical team, more participants were added from other functions viz. QC, QA, IT, Estimation, Marketing and Finance department respectively.

The management review process required Top management to periodically review the QMS to ensure its continuing suitability, adequacy, effectiveness and alignment with the strategic direction of the organization.

Internal and external issues were discussed and resolved- and their potential effect on the strategic direction of the organization analysed.

Mostly the emphasis was making team more responsible with accountability & developing more and more serving hand to strengthen organization.

The management review focus was mainly on the following :

  • Risks and opportunities

    Possible changes that might affect the system

    Customer satisfaction and perception

    Internal Audit results

    Non-conformity and corrective actions

    Execution team and QC team performance review.

    Future Projections and goals

Final outcome of the management Q4 review meeting was continual improvement of the QMS / processes and overall business growth ensuring customer satisfaction.

As our organization's QMS increases in its effectiveness and efficiency, and overall performance will likewise increase.
