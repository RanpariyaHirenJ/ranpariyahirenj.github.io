History Committee President's Message

Shri. Gautam ChatterjeePresident Bengal Club, ShivajiPark, Mumbai.

Dear All,
Let me, at the outset, express my heartfelt thanks to my dear members for giving a clear mandate to our Poriborton Panel members during the recently concluded Executive Committee Elections. We have accepted the responsibility entrusted to us with all humility and would strive to fulfil the same in a collaborative manner with complete integrity, utmost sincerity and full transparency.

I am also taking this opportunity to extend a very hearty welcome to our new Trustee Mrs. Arati Chakraborty. As you may be aware, the other two Trustees of Bengal Club are Dr. Pranab Biswas and Dr. Mrs.Vijaya Chowdhury.

As we embark upon this onerous task of Poriborton, let me share with you some areas where we have already made a humble beginning:

  • The Club website http://bengalclubshivajipark.com/ is getting updated and will now be extensively used to disseminate information regarding Club events and to communicate regularly with our members. We would request you to visit our website at frequent intervals to get the updates.
  • In the first meeting of the Executive Committee held on 20th April, we decided to form six Sub-committees covering all aspects of the functioning of the Club, with wide representation of members from outside the Executive Committee, including youth and lady members.
  • We have firmed up the renovation plan for the Club House and our architect Mr. Redkar is already in the process of submitting the plans to BMC for approval.

Friends, this is just the beginning. We promise you that while we would look forward to welcoming your constructive suggestions, our coherent team of Executive and Sub-committee members will leave no stone unturned to make all of us members feel proud of being a part of a vibrant Bengal Club.

With warm greetings for NoboBorsho 1422.

Gautam Chatterjee
President
