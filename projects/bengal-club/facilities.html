Cricket Coaching Indoor Games Zumba

As part of Sports activities , Bengal Club started Cricket matches and organized tournaments at its Shivaji Park ground. In the year 2001-2002, Club initiated various sports activities aggressively under its dream project for budding sports persons and started Cricket Coaching and is moving from strength to strength. The concept of Bengal Club Sports Academy was incubated during this period and finally shaped in 2009. The Club has Cricket Academy which not only provides coaching to budding cricketers and also host various tournaments approved by Mumbai Cricket Association.
