Software Implementation Manager

Primary Responsibilities

  • Take new clients and transform them into Nexus experts and evangelists
  • Manage all aspects of implementation projects including client satisfaction, project scope, change requests, project status tracking, coordination of internal tasks, and reporting of project KPIs on a weekly and monthly basis.
  • Collaborate with all levels of the LaborEdge organization to connect clients with Product Managers, integration team and Customer Success Managers to further client success.
  • Communicate and document the implementation statuses to management.

Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree (or equivalent work experience), preferably in business or related field
  • 5+ years of relevant experience implementing solutions, driving customer success and engagement, and helping to solve technical problems
  • 3+ years in a management/lead role overseeing software implementations
  • Strong organizational, analytical, and project management skills
  • Ability to multitask and handle multiple projects simultaneously
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills are required, including the ability to work with customers and internal stakeholders
