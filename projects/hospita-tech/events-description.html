Gathering of Hospital IT Leaders

Speaker:
Mr. Mahesh Shinde

Venue:
4th Floor, Conference Hall, Hinduja Clinic Building, Hinduja Hospital Veer Savarkar Marg, Mahim, Mumbai

Home/

Events

It gives us immense pleasure to take up the opportunity to host the first ever formal gathering of Hospital IT Leaders. The event will be held at Hotel Sahara Star, Ville Parle, Mumbai from 5:00 pm to 8:00 pm followed by dinner on 8th August, 2015
