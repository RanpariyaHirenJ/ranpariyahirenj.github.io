ABOUT US

We are a unique team of Spanish linguists that come from prestigious institutions such as Instituto Cervantes and universities across the world such as Oxford University, Reading University, Universidad Antonio de Nebrija, Washington University and Johns Hopkins University amongst others.

We combine expertise in technology and classroom teaching. Our online platform enables group live interaction with students and professors alike as well as individual work (both off and online).

Our professional services are aimed at complete beginners, improvers and those who want to become professionals in teaching the language: we teach the language and we teach how to teach the language! We specialise in teaching Spanish for specific purposes in areas such as health sciences, law, business and tourism.

Our team of professors are experienced examiners of official examinations (Instituto Cervantes’ DELE, SIELE, Madrid Chamber of Commerce and Industry certifications)
