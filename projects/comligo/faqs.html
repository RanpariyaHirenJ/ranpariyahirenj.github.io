FAQs

1. What is a class like?

Our classes are dynamic, hands-on and based on professor and group interaction.

In our classes you will be:

  • Presented with the topic of the lesson
  • Given the necessary language tools to learn the contents
  • Practicing those tools during the class
  • Using the tools to produce the language by yourself
  • Provided with guidance on your learning process
  • Reinforcing your learning through guided activities
2. When will my classes take place?

We offer classes on a 24/7 basis.

3. Who are my teachers?

See in our Who are we? section

4. What equipment do I need?

A computer, a good internet connection, and log onto our platform. It can’t be easier!
