CyberMaster 3D Eminent

CyberMaster 3D Eminent is an advanced Ship Loading software integrated with several Management Tools.

Play Video
  • CyberMaster 3D Eminent can perform all necessary operations pertaining to vessel stability and strength monitoring and reporting.
  • Software can also generate several reports: Bunker, Cargo loaded, Cargo discharged, Various port reports
  • Works on all windows-based Desktops/Laptops and mobile devices
  • Cloud based data transmission from ship to shore and other operation centers.
  • The Fleet Controller Version enables the shore office to view the real time floating status of the different vessel of the entire fleet with its stability and strength results.
  • As the loading condition changes on-board, the same gets updated in the shore office enabling live monitoring.
  • Cargo Loaded & Discharged Analytics facilitates the shore office to view the amount of cargo on-board in different vessel of the fleet.
  • Port wise Cargo Loaded & Discharged enables the shore office to view the amount of cargo loaded and discharge by each vessel in different ports.
  • Fuel on-board feature enables the shore office to view the amount of fuel on-board different vessel of the same fleet. The amount of fuel oil received and consumed within a specified time period can be displayed in Eminent. The types of fuels used on board and their respective quantities can be easily comprehended from the pie chart.
