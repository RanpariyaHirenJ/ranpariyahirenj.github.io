Customization

Customizing CyberMaster3D to serve vessel specific requirements

  • CyberMaster3D is customized with ship specific data comprising the following
    • Hydrostatics
    • KN
    • Tank geometry & Tank Soundings
    • Down flooding Points
    • Tank Categorisation such as Cargo Tanks, FO Tanks etc.
  • It is a very time consuming and arduous task and often gets aggravated due to lack of adequate drawings.
  • Our expertise in generating hull form from very minimal data have enabled us to successfully deliver CyberMaster3D to vessels built in as early as 1990.
