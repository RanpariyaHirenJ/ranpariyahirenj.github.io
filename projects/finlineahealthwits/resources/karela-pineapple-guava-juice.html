Karela, Pineapple,
Guava Juice

Ingredients YIELD - 1 Serving (200ml)

  • 30ml Healthwits Karela Juice
  • 20g or 4 medium pcs pineapple
  • 4 slices guava
  • 4 slices apple
  • 2 tbsp honey or as desired
  • 100ml water
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Method

In a blender, add all the ingredients and juice well and serve chilled
