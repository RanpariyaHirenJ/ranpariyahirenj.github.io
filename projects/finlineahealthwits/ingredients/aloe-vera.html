Aloe Vera -
the wonder plant

Aloe Vera is called a "Wonder Plant", because of its immense health benefits and healing properties.

It owes its healing properties to its rich composition of minerals, enzymes, amino acids, natural sugars and bio active compounds.

Benefits of Aloe Vera

  • Improves digestion

    Aloe Vera helps in detoxifying and smooth functioning of the digestive tract. ( 4 )

  • Antioxidant

    Specific constituents from Aloe Vera help in reducing excess free radicals in the body. ( 1 )

  • Immune booster

    Polysaccharides in Aloe Vera help in improving the immune-boosting properties. ( 2 , 4 )

  • Vitamin E

    It helps in reducing damage caused by excess free radicals, thereby making it an excellent antioxidant. ( 7 )

  • Healing properties

    Polysaccharides present in Aloe Vera increases collagen synthesis and thereby helps in wound healing. ( 6 )

  • Antibacterial

    Studies suggest Aloe Vera leaf gel has several antibacterial components that can inhibit the growth of certain bacteria. These bacteria may be a threat to human health. ( 3 )

  • Relieves stress

    Aloe Vera is helpful in smooth functioning of the body machinery. It reduces cell-damaging process during stressful condition and minimises biochemical and physiological changes in the body.   ( 5 )
