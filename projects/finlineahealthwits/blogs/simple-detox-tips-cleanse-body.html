Simple detox tips to
cleanse your body

Detoxification is the process of removing toxic substances from the body, renewing your ability to maintain optimum health and protecting you from diseases. Every now and then, our body gets exposed to various chemical toxins. The excess amount of toxins accumulated in the body can cause many issues like fatigue, headache, hormonal disruptions, congestion, and inflammatory conditions etc. making it very important to get rid of these toxins.

How does it work?

Basically, detoxification cleanses the blood by removing impurities. The impurities are removed with the help of the liver, intestines, kidneys, lungs, skin and the lymphatic system. The detoxification process accelerates the natural cleansing process of the body. It improves the circulation of the blood, providing the body with important nutrients and minerals.

When do you need it?

Everyone should go through the detoxification process at least once a year. However, people with chronic diseases like tuberculosis or cancer, or nursing mothers should consult their doctor first before trying any detoxification process at home.

The common symptoms that represent the need for a detoxification process include allergies, fatigue, irritation of the skin, puffy eyes, menstrual problems, bloating, or a low-grade infection. If you are going through any of the above-mentioned symptoms, ask your health care provider for a detoxification process.

Here are a few health tips to help you start with

  • Right Diet

    For the detoxification process, it is very important that you select the right diet. Choose fruits and vegetables over junk or any other processed food. Stay several feet away from any contaminated food thereby decreasing the toxin level in the body. Also, add foods to your diet that help in the detoxification process like lemon, mung beans, raw vegetables, garlic, and broccoli sprouts. Drink healthy juices, it is one of the simplest ways to cleanse the body. You can buy healthy juices online and refine your search result as per your choices.

  • Limit Sugar

    Start removal of toxins by limiting the amount of sugar intake. It includes all artificial sweeteners, molasses and honey. Eating more sugar causes your body to ask for more insulin. This causes strain on your pancreas and you will just wear yourself out. It may also lead to weight gain, increasing the risk of problems like constant fatigue and diabetes.

  • Meditate daily

    It will help you clean your mind and body. It will also reduce stress. Stress is like a toxin to your mind. A troubled mind can cause trouble in your body as well.

  • Exercise daily

    as it will make you sweat and sweat removes toxins from the body through the skin. Exercising improves digestion, strengthens the body, lubricates the joints, and reduces tension. People who exercise daily have lower levels of toxins in the body.

    During the detoxification process, it is important that you breathe fresh air. It is impossible to control the air outside your home. However, you can control the air inside your home. Get some house plants that are natural air fresheners. Use a Neti pot before going to bed to flush your nasal passages. It will help you sleep and breathe better and remove the side effects of air pollution. You can also try some oil massages and skin brushing. Exfoliation will increase the circulation of the blood, removing the toxins from the skin.

    Water is the most important tool in the detoxification process. It helps flush the toxins out of your body, thus getting rid of chemicals that are diminishing the natural glow of your skin. Water also hydrates the skin, making the cells healthier from the inside, thus causing your skin to look healthy and feel soft and supple.

Detoxification is a step by step process that removes toxins from every organ present in the body. If your body shows severe symptoms of the presence of toxins in the body, you should go for a program that removes all toxins, chemicals and harmful substances from your liver, kidney, and colon.
