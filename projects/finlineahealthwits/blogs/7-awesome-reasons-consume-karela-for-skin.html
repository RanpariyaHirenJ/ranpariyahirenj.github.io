7 awesome reasons to consume
Karela for your skin

For the better part of our lives, we have absolutely hated the bitter taste of karela. Even though we are well aware its benefits outweigh its taste, we have never been a fan of the bitter gourd. Bitter gourd, also known as bitter melon, is popular for its innumerable benefits to achieve glowing, healthy skin.

It has been a natural ingredient to numerous juice cleanses, healthy vegetable juices, detox foods, and face packs due to its various healing properties, health benefits, and high nutritional value.

Here are a few awesome reasons to pick up some Karelas on your next grocery spree

  • It is an excellent antioxidant

    A cup of bitter gourd juice is known to contain up to 26 mg of vitamin C. Vitamin C is a powerful detoxifier and also helps boost production of collagen. It protects cells from further oxidation and balances the body's toxic levels. It helps purify blood and stimulate proper regulation of hormones. Impure blood and hormones are the top causes of acne, dark spots, and scarring.

  • It helps keep skin healthy

    The blood purifying ability of bitter gourd helps keep skin, in general, very healthy. Karela, as a regular part of one's diet, can help fight skin issues such as eczema and psoriasis. It keeps acne at bay and helps give your skin a natural healthy glow. Bitter gourd contains anti-microbial properties that help purify the blood. This, in turn, helps cure problems such as blood boils, ringworm, and other bacterial and fungal skin diseases.

  • Helps in controlling dandruff

    Dandruff is caused due to many reasons, and it is a common problem. Bitter gourd can help reduce dandruff. A mix of bitter gourd juice and cumin seeds paste soaked in water can help fight dandruff. Using this hair pack regularly over a period of 2 months can help fight dandruff completely. Regularly applying bitter gourd juice mixed with curd as a conditioner also helps in reducing hair fall.

  • Cures heat rashes

    A paste of boiled bitter gourd can be applied to the affected area or else, water in which the bitter gourd is boiled, can be mixed with bathing water for excellent results in controlling heat rashes and body acne. It helps cool down the skin and repair sun-damaged skin. Applying bitter gourd juice mixed with lemon onto your skin also helps remove tan and protects your skin from UV rays.

  • Helps reduce blemishes due to acne

    Acne is one of the most common skin problems. Acne can often leave scars and blemishes that may sometimes take years to fade away. Drinking bitter gourd juice every morning on an empty stomach can help reduce the scars, giving you a blemish free skin. It also helps improve complexion by detoxifying the skin. Applying masks of bitter gourd regularly can help keep skin plump and healthy naturally.

  • Anti-Aging properties

    Bitter Gourd is very popular for its anti-ageing properties. It is loaded with vitamin A and Vitamin C which help reduce fine lines, wrinkles, and prevent premature ageing. Direct application of bitter gourd juice to the scalp on a weekly basis can help make hair healthier, shinier, and younger looking.

  • Weight loss

    Besides all these beauty benefits, bitter gourd has a high antioxidant content which helps in losing weight. Bitter gourd juice, if consumed daily helps in losing weight, as it increases your metabolism and helps with the digestive process. A diet that is full of high fibre fruits and vegetables complemented with bitter gourd juice helps in losing weight due to the high fibre and low-calorie content.
