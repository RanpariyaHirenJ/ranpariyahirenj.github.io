Things to do if you are
tired all the time

  • Are you tired all the time?

Getting more than 8 hours of sleep at night but still feeling tired the next day? Feeling wiped out all the time is a very common issue these days. One among 10 people say that they feel tired most of the time. The working class is the most affected one. Struggling between work and personal life, these people often refuse to consider fatigue as a legitimate disease. As it is a side effect of depression, stress or anxiety, there is no test that can be used to diagnose fatigue.

Everybody gets tired at some point or the other. However, if you stay tired all day long and are unable to perform daily tasks,

here are a few tips that will help you in overcoming fatigue

  • Say Goodbye to Stress

    Stress is the main reason behind fatigue. It increases your blood pressure and slow down your digestive system, soaking up all the energy from the body. You can try some stress reduction techniques that will give you energy and also boost your mood. These include deep breathing, meditation, yoga, etc.

  • Change your Lifestyle

    Begin the day with some meditation and Yoga and a healthy breakfast. This will motivate you and help you overcome fatigue. Avoid junk food. Begin your day with healthy juices. Eat a well-balanced, nutritious diet with all the proteins, complex carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals. Drink lots of water, as dehydration can cause energy loss. Eat adequate calories so that you have enough energy to perform your tasks. Drinking healthy drinks will not only help you stay hydrated but will also amp up your nutrition level.

  • I, Me and Myself

    Never ignore yourself. Give yourself as much time, care and energy as you give others. When people don't take care of themselves, they start feeling resentful. Do anything that you love to do like, getting a massage, taking a long walk alone or any other hobby. Try to maintain a journal where you can pour out all your feelings and emotions.

  • Rewarding yourself

    This is another way of taking care of yourself. You should always acknowledge your success. This helps in rebuilding motivation and ultimately getting rid of fatigue. Rewards will motivate you to continue. Just keep in mind that these rewards are not given randomly. Specify a goal and when you have achieved that goal, give yourself a treat. However, rewarding yourself with a big meal, if your goal was to exercise or eat healthy, will go against the purpose.

  • Set a goal

    Decide on a goal and write it down. Pin it in a place where you can see it and feel motivated. Prepare a plan to achieve that goal. Prioritize your tasks and begin from the easiest one. Don't go too fast. Remember that slow and steady wins the race. Don't set unrealistic and unachievable goals. Be specific and give yourself a deadline to finish that goal.

  • Good Night's sleep

    Sleep is a kind of a repair process that we do to our body. It is the most important tip if you want to stay productive. Make your room sleep-friendly to sleep better at night. The room should be quiet, dark and cool. Avoid coffee after dinner. Avoid using your mobile before bed and keep it 2 feet away from the bed, as radiations cause our brain activity to increase and disturb our sleep.

  • A Healthy Diet

    Avoid food that has a high content of refined sugar. Excess of processed food will drain your energy and make you fat and flabby, leaving you lethargic. Go for unprocessed foods like unsweetened dairy products, fruits, vegetables, and nuts. You can try healthy fresh juices. Healthy drinks are an important part of the healthy diet as they are easy to make and also bring a sense of Zen in lifestyle.

During the whole process, it is very important that you motivate yourself continuously. Believe in yourself. It will help you set your goals and work on it. Tiredness is a natural thing to occur. However, if you feel tired all the time or at regular intervals, it is not normal. So, just don't blow it off. If all these tips don't work for you, consult your physician to find the cause behind your prolonged tiredness.
