OUR TREATMENTS

  • 01. Invisalign

    The Invisalign System is a comfortable, predictable and virtually invisible treatment to effectively straighten your teeth while still letting you do the things you love.

    Learn More

  • 02. Insignia

    Insignia is advanced software that your orthodontist uses to develop a treatment plan that is tailored to each individual patient providing fast, accurate and predicable results.

    Learn More

  • 03. Incognito (Lingual)

    Incognito braces are contoured to hug the lingual (tongue-side) surfaces of your teeth. These braces are customised with intelligent wires and brackets that are engineered to deliver effective results.

    Learn More

  • 04. Ceramic (Clear) Braces

    Ceramic (Clear) braces are virtually invisible. These aesthetic braces are colour matched to your particular tooth colour so they don't stand out making them as discreet as possible.

    Learn More

  • 05. Functional appliances

    There are two functional appliances available when your upper or lower jaw does not align properly; these are removable functional appliances or the fixed functional appliances.

    Learn More

  • 06. Non-extraction

    With modern orthodontic techniques, it is often possible to treat moderate crowding or protrusion without removing any teeth. At Fox Orthodontics we have been pioneers in non-extraction treatment and strongly believe in having a harmonious face and a beautiful smile.

    Learn More
