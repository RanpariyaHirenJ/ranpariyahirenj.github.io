Invisalign employs a series of plastic aligners that are customised to align your teeth in a phased manner.

Unlike braces which are fixed, these aligners are easy to remove when you want to clean or eat, and are comfortable on your teeth. Invisalign treatment allows you to smile in confidence and visualise each stage of the treatment process, from the beginning to the end, giving you a clearer picture of the treatment duration. It is easier to clean your teeth and maintain oral health during treatment with Invisalign. So if you have an active lifestyle or special occasion coming up, then Invisalign may be the right choice for you.
