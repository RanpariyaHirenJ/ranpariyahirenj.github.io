Our Staff

The success of Fox Orthodontics is due to the
dedication and expertise of all our staff.

Karen is a friendly hardworking team member at our clinic that has been with us for the past 14 years. Karen is the voice on the other end of the phone and the first friendly face that greets you on your arrival at the clinic.

Karen’s passion for the dental field led her straight from school to begin her career at the United Dental Hospital. She has also worked for numerous general dental clinics over the years and her experience in the dental field is extensive.

Vanessa is one of our qualified dental assistants that has been with us for the past 15 years, she is a hardworking friendly team member with a wide variety of experience within her expertise in the dental field and is well known for her high level of patient care.

Vanessa started her career at the United Dental Hospital where she completed her Dental Assistant Certificate. Vanessa has worked in various different dental fields where she has gained a vast array of experience that she shares with everyone she comes into contact with at the clinic.

Tracey is one of our dedicated dental assistants that has been with our practice for the past 6 years. She is a hardworking friendly team member that has excellent typing and clinical experience.

She is dedicated to all areas of the practice and brings a variety of skills that help the clinic run smoothly. Tracey has worked in a number of orthodontic clinics over the years where she gained valuable experience that she utilises in the practice every day.
