Hassle free
Asset Disposal

At OPC, we are conscious of e-waste being a major environmental issue worldwide. E-waste can have concentrations of lead, mercury and other harmful chemicals that leach into soil and groundwater. That's why dumping old IT equipment into landfill can cause long term damage to the environment.

We have made it a policy to dispose old IT equipment in a completely environment friendly way. We collect your old computers, destroy the hard drive, and recycle it.

If you're upgrading your existing infrastructure, we'll collect the old computers no longer you want, and find them a new home.
