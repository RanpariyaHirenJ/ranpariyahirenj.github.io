Asset Life Cycle Management

The management of an asset throughout its lifecycle requires planning and support for the investment decision. This includes acquisition, access and ongoing maintenance, through to replacement or retirement planning.

The objective of OPCs customized asset lifecycle management plans is to optimize asset acquisition, with its Core Rental plans, SWAP plan, Sale and Rent Back plan for maintenance upgrade, handling disposal through re-deployment and other effective solutions to maximize the use of the asset. This would reduce associated service and operational costs, resulting in increased asset performance and a lower Total Cost of Ownership.

OPC's customized services for effective lifecycle management

  • Core rental services comprises of standard plans and flexible customized plans
  • Asset management services comprises of Swap plan, Sale & Rent Back plan including asset tagging and warranty
  • Re-deployment and E-waste disposal for end of cycle asset management
