About OPC

OPC Asset Solutions is a leading independent player in the equipment leasing market in India.

Mission

Our Mission is to be the most innovative and preferred partner for operating leases in maximum asset classes.

Vision

Our Vision is to be a top most trusted leader and partner with flexible, customized rental solutions, and allow organizations to access the latest technology and infrastructure.

At OPC, we strive to be the best by meeting every asset need with our Asset Life Cycle Management.

Our dedication has rewarded us with 100% Client retention, at an astounding growth rate. We approach clients with total transparency offering a lifecycle solution, with support all along.

Founded in2005

With an aim to partner clients and provide perfect combinations of Asset Domain Expertise and Structured Financing Solutions. OPC Asset Solutions was set up by a group of far sighted professionals with over six decades of experience in various domains ranging from IT, Payment Solutions, Manufacturing, Financial Services and Turnkey EPC (engineering, procurement & construction).

Our end to end Asset Lifecycle Solutions are tailored to suit the client's unique requirement. We make sure that we take care of every infrastructural need while clients are left free to focus on their core business. We help clients retain their competitive edge by empowering them to keep up with the latest technology and infrastructure without worrying about huge investments.
