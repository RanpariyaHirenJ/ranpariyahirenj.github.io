  • Swaplan

Swaplan is the best rental solutions in the market. It is a unique proposition that combines the Dual Advantage Scheme with a phased Swap Upgrade process. You can sequentially phase your current depreciating assets and rented equipment in an ordered, synchronized fashion. Its high flexibility allows you to continue operating at peak efficiency even while your core business equipment is sent for maintenance upgrade.

With Swaplan

  • You can replace your initial rental equipment with new equipment at different phases of the term

  • You can also swap your existing technology with no escalation in the payment; just an extension in the duration of payments, corresponding to the value of the swap.

  • You can choose to increase your payment and maintain the duration.
