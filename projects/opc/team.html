R. Venkatesan | Managing Director

R. Venkatesan has over 25 years experience in finance, planning, controlling and auditing various businesses in India, Africa and South East Asia. As Managing Director of OPC, he is responsible for the overall management of business and strategic initiatives.

Before OPC, Venkatesan worked with Dell India and was responsible for Business Planning, Controllership, Reporting, Taxation and Compliance.

Parag Kapasi | Executive Director

Parag Kapasi is the Executive Director of OPC and has over 20 years experience in Sales and Marketing financial services in India. He is responsible for Strategic Alliances, structuring / BD and Asset Lifecycle Management.

Earlier, Parag was Head of Business Develoment for Euronet India and was responsible for acquisition of a multi-million dollar business for the company.

Viraf Bodhanwala | Director - Treasury, Business Finance, And Operations

With over 18 years of experience as a Finance Professional, Viraf has extensive experience in Treasury, Corporate Finance, Risk Management, Budgeting & MIS. He is responsible for Treasury, Business Finance, Legal and Operations functions in OPC.

Earlier, Viraf worked as a CFO in TVS Infrastructure Ltd and took care of Finance, Commercial, Legal & Secretarial, Accounting, Projects & all other functions. He was also associated with Glenmark, Novartis & Jet Airways.

Jagannath Sabat | CFO

Jagannath has over 17 years of experience in managing a wide gamut of financial and commercial functions across diverse industries. At OPC, he is responsible for Corporate Strategy, financial planning and reporting, treasury management, controllership, secretarial function, taxation, compliance, and IT.

Before OPC, he worked with Glenmark, Moser Baer and Euronet taking care of various functions under finance, Audit, Commercial and Compliance verticals
