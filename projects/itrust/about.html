About

What’s important to you when it comes to tax?

iTrust Tax and Accountants was formed by a team of professional and qualified accountants who saw the need to help others get better tax results without the headaches and lack of accountability often experienced in the market place.

Senior Partner Rizwan says:

A lot of people come to us who have had problems meeting their ATO tax deadlines, have received tax fines and lacked forward planning in anticipation of future tax liabilities. It all comes down to forward thinking, intelligent tax advice and giving people who work hard for their money a fair go.

Professional experience in tax, accounting and financial services.

After working with a top tier accounting firm helping corporate and managing directors file their returns, reduce their risk and maximise their tax results he joined a small accounting firm and helped them double their revenue base while providing advanced strategic tax advice to small/ medium sized businesses, professionals and investors.

With a combined resource of chartered accountants, degree qualified and licenced advisers, iTrust provides you with tax and wealth management services tailored to suit your individual and business needs.

Your consultant will be degree qualified with a minimum of 10 years experience in accounting, tax and or financial planning services.

Our tax agent services are conveniently located in Macquarie Park, North Ryde, Epping, Sydney.
