We are SYMA

Driven by customer obsession and delivery excellence.

We don't chase success, we chase excellence. We are a boutique company helping our clients achieve Business Agility powered by SAFe and AWS. We want to be focused on, committed to and obsessed with our goal. We are investing into building deep expertise in AWS Big Data, Advanced Analytics, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence capabilities. SYMA is born in Australia for the Australian companies aiming for business agility and digital transformation.

Founder and Director

Satish Agrawal

Education

He is an XBA®, SPC5®, CLP®, PSM2®, PSM1®, PSPO1®, CSP®, CSM®, PRINCE2®, ITIL®, MCP® (Microsoft Project 2013) certified Agile Coach

Global Exposure

Satish has worked with global enterprises such as NAB (Melbourne), Telstra Corp (Melbourne), Bupa (Melbourne), Tabcorp Holdings Ltd. (Melbourne), Qwest Communications (Denver), British Telecom (Belfast), Wells Fargo Bank (San Francisco), Essent Energy (Amsterdam), Carlson Wagonlit Travel (Paris), McGraw Hill Education (New York), Mount Sinai Hospital (New York) helping cross-functional and multi-skilled teams in complex global delivery models, using both Waterfall (phased gate) and Agile Project Management Processes to deliver value.

Current Focus

Satish has worked as Agile Coach, Scrum Master and Agile Delivery Manager helping teams on the journey to continuous improvement using Kaizen, self-reflections and gamifications.

Satish has Solid experience in delivering projects and product increments in agile environment using Scrum and SAFe frameworks, conducting agile events such as release planning, iteration planning, product backlog refinement, conducting retrospective and showcases, helping teams in agile estimations using poker planning, breaking down large epics into more manageable stories.

Satish is an open book as his close friends call him. He finished his M.Sc. in Computer Science in 1995 from the Post Graduate Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Amravati University, Amravati. He has been working relentlessly since then in IT and the Software Engineering Industry and has played many roles and worn many hats. He has worked in India, US, UK, Europe and Australia.
