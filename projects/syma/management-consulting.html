Consultancy Services

Our IT Projects and Program Management Consultancy Services brings optimised business value to your enterprise using PMI’s proven Project and Program Management frameworks. We deliver outcomes and help enterprises build a culture, based on delivering commitment on time and within budget.

Our projects and program management professionals with decades of experience delivering on multi-million dollar projects will help you through all phases of the projects and program lifecycle. We also help in setting up a PMO for you and develop a framework to measure the success of the projects and programs. Whether your enterprise is working in predictive or adaptive ways, our consultants will provide you the right tools and techniques to fit your purpose.

Delivery Framework

We have developed a unique program delivery framework that has a fine blend of traditional and modern program delivery methodologies balancing your enterprise’s need for predictive project management, yet capable of adapting to the new ways of working. Our experts in Agile Transformation will help you transition from traditional ways of working to lean agile ways of working with minimal disruption to your existing processes. We have a ‘people first’ approach when it comes to change management and transformation!
