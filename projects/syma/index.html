What does a cloud consultant do?

Broadly speaking, an AWS CLoud Consultant designs, develops, implements, tests the automating system and application deployments on AWS, collaborates with other teams to keep a check on the proposed architecture and suggests changes if necessary.

A Cloud Consultant

  • Automates cloud migration from on-premise business applications to AWS cloud
  • Gathers and evaluates user and business requirements
  • Develop specifications/designs
  • Helps clients to improve business performance by penetrating deeper in Cloud / Emerging technology area

How do I get a SAFe Agile certification?

A SAFe Agile certification can be acquired by sitting for an exam. This is a tough exam and without experience and training, it is difficult to crack it. Scaled Agile, Inc. is the body that certified the Scaled Agile Framework® (SAFe®) which is recognised globally and certified professionals are sought after for their skills in transforming organisations into Lean Agile enterprises.

To become a SAFe certified professional, a candidate can start by joining a professional course (usually conducted over 2-3 days) which initiates and prepares one into a reliable and consistent method of assessing SAFe skills and knowledge. The candidate is then signed up for the SAFe Agilist online test conducted by Scaled Agile. To pass the test, the candidate has to score 75%. Once the candidate clears the exam, he or she becomes a SAFe Agile certified professional.

Is it a good idea to use a staffing agency?

Staffing agencies help companies find the right talent as they have access jobs in a large number of companies in different industries, which are otherwise not easy to find through job portals etc.

Using a staffing agency can help in saving time that is spent in reviewing innumerable resumes, shortlisting candidates, etc. They offer a high level of expertise which HR departments find hard to match.

It also saves money as a staffing agency is experienced and can provide better candidates and placements in lesser time, than an inhouse team. Hence the company can save on training and onboarding too.

A staffing agency takes over the hassle of handling contractor payroll and worker issues like unemployment benefits.
