WILDLIFE CONSERVATION

“The approach of the artist and the approach of the environmentalist are fairly close in that both are, to a rather impressive degree, concerned with the affirmation of life.” - Ansel Adams (1902-1984)

Wildlife photography should not be the exclusive privilege of our generation. My mission is to help conserve wildlife through the viewfinder of my camera. All proceeds from the sale of my photographs help fund NGOs that are actively working in the area of wildlife conservation.

Photography is my passion. Conservation is my purpose.
