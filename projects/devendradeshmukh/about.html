A SNAPSHOT ON ME

LIFE BEHIND THE CAMERA

Devendra is an award winning nature photographer, specializing in wildlife photography. Devendra is pursuing wildlife photography as a means to document wildlife and highlight the importance & efforts required for its conservation. His passion for challenges manifests itself through his love for adventurous activities like trekking, cycling& travelling across continents in search of wildlife. He had traveled to global locations like Ecuador, Botswana, Japan, Kenya, India & South Africa. He is committed to wildlife conservation and supports various NGOs that work in the area of wildlife protection.

Devendra is a successful technology entrepreneur and venture partner with Fresco Capital - a global early-stage venture investor. He also a Charter Member of TiE Pune Chapter. He mentors upcoming entrepreneurs and is frequently invited to deliver lectures on leadership, entrepreneurship at various forums and conferences. For his dynamic leadership and contribution, he was recently awarded the Rajiv Gandhi Excellence in IT Award.
