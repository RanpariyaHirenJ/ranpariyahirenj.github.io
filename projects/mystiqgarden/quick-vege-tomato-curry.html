Quick vege tomato curry

Ingredients

½ head of broccoli (approx. 200g), cut into florets ¼ cauliflower (approx. 200g), cut into florets ½ bag green beans (approx. 150g), sliced (alternative is to use frozen beans) 14 button mushrooms (130g) 1 can Indian flavoured tomatoes 2 Tbsp tomato paste 2 tsp garam masala (optional)

Method

  • Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
  • Add a little water, broccoli and cauliflower. Steam for 3 minutes. Add beans, mushrooms, canned tomatoes, and tomato puree (to thicken it).
  • Add garam masala if you prefer a less sweet tasting dish. Cook 5 minutes until tender. Quick vege tomato curry Serves 4 Tip: change the flavour of the canned tomatoes to have the dish a different way, eg. Moroccan or Mexican. To turn into a full meal, serve with rice and chicken.

Energy

Energy 252kJ, Fat 0.8g, Saturated fat 0.1g, Carbohydrate 8g, Fibre 4.8g, Sodium 126mg
