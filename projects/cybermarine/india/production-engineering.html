Production Engineering

  • View Projects Gallery

Over the years being in the field of Ship Design business, Cybermarine understands the requirement of providing entire package of ship design services to its valued customers. We provide a comprehensive range of value added Detailed Engineering & Production Drawings preparation services for our customers to support their new-building, conversion, modification and refit projects. The group comprising of 3D Modellers and Draftsmen with hands on experience in “Production Engineering of Ships & Rigs” guided by experienced Naval Architects with an ability to support any building projects for both Marine and Offshore Industry. Our strength lies in our domain competence and expertise, supported by a skilled and experienced team of 3D Modellers and draftsmen, who are familiar with all Global engineering standards, proficient in cutting edge 3D tools and conversant with updated technologies. We leverage our domain expertise to deliver a unique value proposition to our customers.

A good number of globally reputed, shipbuilders, Rig/platform builders from different parts of world viz: middle-east, Singapore / APAC, Europe and USA, have already shown enormous interest to work with us with a long term vision. We have already worked with customers from Norway, Netherlands, Srilanka, Kenya, UAE & India.

Complete Production Documentation Package

Hull / Structure
  • Creation of unit division plan
  • Preparation of Hull Surface
  • Entire modeling of hull structure, accommodation, appendages including major foundations as per the unit division plan
  • Unit wise Production Drawing & Assembly Drawings / sequences
  • Preparation of Shell / curve plate bending templates
  • Profile Sketches with dimensional drawings
  • Preparation of detailed bill of material of plates and stiffeners
  • Preparation of Nesting plan
  • Development of CNC cutting programs
  • Weight and COG
Miscellaneous Services
  • Feasibility Study and Design support for Vessel lengthening & Conversion
  • Assistance in Structural Analysis & Design
  • Support in supervision
  • Project Planning – technical & Financial
Machinery, Piping, Electrical & HVAC
  • Creation of zone / compartment plan
  • Creation of machinery and equipment models & component libraries
  • Entire outfit modeling including machineries, piping systems in all areas, electrical cable trays & Conduits, Cable Ladders & HVAC system including ducts
  • Generation of zone-wise / compartment-wise Production arrangement drawings including structure as reference and showing impression of all equipment / machineries, pipes, cable ladders, ducts etc
  • Preparation of pipe spool drawings (40NB & Above)
  • Preparation of Steel outfitting shop drawings, door plan, window plan, staircase/ladder plan, handrail & floor plating plan
  • Foundation modeling and seat drawings for equipment more than 50 kg weight
  • Preparation of complete BOM for fitting, spools, components and compartments, zone-wise, compartment-wise or system wise
