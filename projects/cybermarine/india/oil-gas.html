FEED Studies

    We have carried out FEED studies on:

  • MODU - MOPU conversions
  • MODU - Offshore Installation conversions
  • Upgrading MODU to harsher environments/ higher elevated loads
  • Classification / Reclassification of MODU/MOPU
  • Increasing Operability of Tender Barges

    FEED studies covered all the domains of Naval Architecture, Structural Engineering, Mechanical, Electrical and Instrumentation engineering. These studies also considered all aspects of layout and safety engineering.
