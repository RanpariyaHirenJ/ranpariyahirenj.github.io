  • About Us

    Cybermarine is a leading Design & Engineering Enterprise with global operations.

Headquartered in Singapore and controls the worldwide operations through the offices at Houston, Kuala Lumpur and Mumbai.

Cybermarine as a company built from scratch, has developed innovative technology and processes over the years and is in a position to deliver wide ranging design solutions to the Oil & Gas and Marine industry. Cybermarine, by utilizing its proficiency in engineering & extensive design experience has successfully delivered a range of design and engineering solutions on Jack-up Rigs, Semi-submersibles and FPSOs. We have also successfully completed several designs for many types of Cargo Vessels, Offshore support vessels, Passenger Vessels and landing crafts.

Projects are executed using innovative and well-developed technology, which include Work Break-Down, Design-Spirals and 3D Space Arrangements/Models. The execution is augmented by well laid-down processes consisting of work specification spreadsheets and process checklists. Efficient work is enabled by extensive macros and well developed libraries in various software that are used.

Our technical teams are organized as project teams and operations' teams. Project teams comprise of project managers and project engineers responsible for project management and client interaction. Project teams are stationed in all our offices at various geographical locations.

Operations' teams comprising of Naval Architects/Structural Engineers/Mechanical & Electrical Engineers/Designers are responsible for project Deliveries and class approvals. They are stationed at Mumbai and Singapore.

FOUNDERS

In the year 1992, two young Naval Architects decided to throw their comfortable jobs and jump into a start-up mode. The idea was to create a tech enterprise where everyone strives for excellence.

This idea took shape in a room in a small apartment where one of them used to live. In 24 years this idea has grown into an organization with 80 technology professionals working in several parts of the world and delivering complex and customized solutions.

The young Naval Architects (no longer young) are our Founders – Bhasker Rao & Arvind Dhupkar. Both hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Naval Architecture from the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras.
