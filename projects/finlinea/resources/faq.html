Faq

  • What are the benefits of Aloe Vera?

    The American Herbal Pharmacopoeia (AHP) monograph on Aloe Vera cited numerous references on health benefits. It helps to improve digestive health and immunity and its antioxidant properties help reduce excess free radicals.

  • What are the benefits of Jamun?

    The Ayurvedic Pharmacopoeia of India monograph states about the therapeutic use of Jamun in diabetes. World Health Organization (WHO) refers to the role of Jamun as a medicinal plant. Various research papers have established the beneficial role of Jamun in balancing blood sugar level. It helps to improve digestive health, has immune boosting properties, and contains antioxidants, which help to reduce excess free radicals.

  • What are the benefits of Karela?

    The Alternative Medicine Review (Vol12) states about the use of Karela in balancing blood sugar level. According to WHO, most of the world's population use Karela as a medicinal plant. Several research studies on Karela indicate that it helps to improve digestive health, contains antioxidants, helps to reduce weight, improves skin health and has wound healing properties.

  • What is Detox?

    The oxford dictionary defines Detox as a process of removing toxic substances. The medical dictionary further elaborates the action of detoxification. It is one of the widely used treatments and concepts of alternative medicine to eliminate existing toxins and avoid new toxins. Detoxification helps in treating various health issues also.

  • What is Cleanse?

    The oxford dictionary defines Cleanse as a process or period of time during which a person attempts to rid the body of substances regarded as toxic or unhealthy, typically by consuming only water or other liquids. Ayurveda advises such cleansing which helps to improve the digestion and metabolic processes.

    Pasteurisation is a process by which food is heated to a specific temperature for a specific amount of time to kill (or deactivate) the number of potentially harmful bacteria.
