Incorporate aloe vera into your skin care routine to answer your skin prayers

Aloe Vera- the magic plant! With a plethora of benefits under its belt, Aloe vera is truly a super-plant. Why? Because this plant has healing powers and isn't hard on your pocket. Aloe vera is exquisitely beneficial for a profusion of things, such as, skin care, hair care, treating burns, anti-itch cream, etc. This versatile plant is high in Vitamin A, C, E, folic acid, choline, niacin, B6, B12 and contains about 20 crucial minerals, such as calcium, magnesium, zinc, iron, etc. Amazing, isn’t it? But, that is not all. It is also rich in amino acids (building blocks of protein) and fatty acids.

Apart from that, aloe vera can also help purify the body, maintain cardiovascular health, boost your immune system, reduce weight, and last but not the least, help achieve lustrous hair and glowing skin. So, how to use aloe vera for a flawless and a glowing complexion? Read on to know more.

  • Makeup Remover

    Removing your makeup immaculately is highly important for good skin. Today, you will come across several makeup removers in the market. However, they can be harsh on your skin and can strip your skin of all its natural oils. But, aloe vera saves the day. It is an excellent makeup remover. It is very cheap, easily available, and nourishes the skin. Just take some aloe vera gel and apply it all over your face. Remove the makeup using a cotton pad or a face towel. You can either use store-bought gel or use the gel directly from the plant. However, if you are buying the gel, make sure it is natural with no added chemicals.

  • Toner

    Using aloe vera, you can make your own soothing toner. This toner will help tighten your pores without taking away any essential oil. In fact, it will provide the necessary hydration. During summers, you can also use this toner as a face mist. And, it is also perfect to set your makeup. The recipe for this toner is very simple. You will need a spray bottle, aloe vera gel, and water. Make sure you use either mineral, distilled, or filtered water. Now, take one teaspoon of aloe vera gel and mix it efficiently with about 70-80 ml of water. Once mixed, store it in your spray bottle. You can also refrigerate it for a cooling effect.

  • Serum

    Aloe vera and lemon serum gives you glowing skin and clears your complexion. This serum is perfect for your nighttime routine as it contains lemon. And, lemon makes your skin photosensitive. For this wonderful serum, you will need about 1-2 tablespoons of aloe vera gel (it can be from the plant or store-bought) and a few drops of lemon. Mix it well and refrigerate it. Use it like any other serum. However, make sure you make a fresh batch, every 3-4 days.

  • Moisturizer

    You can simply use aloe vera gel as a moisturizer. It will make your skin soft, supple, and hydrated. However, if you have dry skin, you can mix coconut oil with the aloe vera gel for that extra nourishment. You can use this moisturizer both on your body and face.

  • Face Wash

    Show your skin some love with this gentle face wash. Apart from brightening the skin, it will add that extra nourishment to keep it looking healthy. For this face wash, you will need aloe vera gel and honey. Use equal parts of both ingredients. And, gently wash your face with it, and you are done.

  • Scrub

    Exfoliating your skin is necessary, but sometimes it can be harsh on the skin. However, aloe vera can be used as a face scrub and thanks to its soothing properties, it will not damage the skin. For scrubbing, mix aloe vera gel and brown sugar or baking soda and gently exfoliate. Then, wash it off with cool water. Do this 1-2 times a week.

  • Acne treatment

    Just apply the aloe vera gel on your blemishes and wait for the magic. Ta-da! Your inflammation will go down, the zits will shrink, and well yeah, goodbye to all the bacteria.

  • Eyebrow Gel

    Instead of investing in expensive eyebrow gels, just turn to aloe vera. The sticky aloe vera gel will keep your brows in place. You can use an old and washed mascara brush to apply it. You can also use aloe vera as an after-shave gel, on cracked heels, and as a hand cream.
    Note: Always do a patch test before using.

  • Benefits of using aloe vera on the skin

    • It has anti-aging properties.
    • It lightens blemishes.
    • Soothes sunburn.
    • Provides hydration and moisturization.
    • Reduces acne.
    • It helps reduce the stretch marks.
    • Reduces tan.
    • Heals Wounds.
