  • Pasteurisation

    Pasteurisation is a process by which foods are heated to a specific temperature for a certain amount of time to eliminate a target number of potentially harmful bacteria.

    Benefits of Pasteurisation:

    1. Eliminates microorganisms
    2. Minimises foodborne diseases
    3. Increases shelf-life

Aspects that make Healthwits Juices the right choice

  • Healthwits Juices are produced using naturally available ingredients specially grown and selected from reliable farms, manufactured and packaged scientifically at our plant in Pune
  • The juices are made using the whole Karela along with its seeds - so no vital and active ingredients are lost
  • The manufacturing plant is operated under the supervision of skilled, trained professionals and staff who are experienced in handling and processing of various products
  • All the ingredients are washed thrice to ensure cleanliness and maintain hygiene standards
  • Juices are extracted in one of the most hygienic and sterile environments achieved through filtered air and stainless steel machinery to ensure the safety of our end product
  • Healthwits Juices are pasteurised to ensure elimination of microbes-causing foodborne diseases and also to increase the shelf-life of the juice
  • Quality check of the end product is conducted and results are verified in accordance with the Food Safety and Quality Policy
  • All our juices are packed in opaque, tamper-resistant PET bottles protecting juices from sunlight and keeping them fresh for long
  • No added sugars
