Privacy Policy

LaborEdge collects and uses your personal information to operate LaborEdge sites and deliver the services you have requested. LaborEdge sites also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from LaborEdge and its affiliates. LaborEdge may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

LaborEdge does not disclose, sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. LaborEdge may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally-identifiable information (email, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party.

LaborEdge keeps track of the websites and pages our customers visit within LaborEdge in order to determine what LaborEdge sites and services are the most popular.

LaborEdge websites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

(a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on LaborEdge or the site;

(b) protect and defend the rights or property of LaborEdge.

For questions about our privacy policy, please email us at info@LaborEdge.com for more information. We welcome your feedback and support efforts to protect your privacy on the internet.
