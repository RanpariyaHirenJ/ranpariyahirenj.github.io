Overview - FinCraftTM banking suite and support services

Over the last two decades Nelito has built deep domain expertise in Banking & insights which are ingrained in our motto: Solutions That Perform. This coupled with our execution excellence and presence covering entire India including remote towns, from Gujarat to Manipur and Jammu to Kerala keeps us close to our customers. Our core value proposition is Affordable excellence.

Banking industry today faces a unique challenge. Incumbent or established banks having created massive technology infrastructure and are facing the challenge of upgrading or overhauling their obsolete and disparate systems having massive cost implications. Optimizing their massive brick-and-mortar branch network's performance and lowering the cost of overall operations remains a challenge.

On the other hand, new banks are faced with the challenge of creating new highly functional and agile systems from scratch which will enable them to compete with the established players. These banks will be relying on mobile and cloud based banking solutions to reach out to the huge market.

Nelito's Solutions

Nelito's comprehensive FinCraftTM banking suite and support services offer significant value addition to Banks through our experience, domain expertise and delivery excellence. Our solutions are available in Opex as well as Capex models. Incumbent banks do not have to depend on solutions from the bigger or multiple solution providers for their evolving requirements; instead they can take advantage of our solutions which provide affordable excellence. Nelito is thus placed well and committed to be an ideal technology partner of trust for the Banks.

Nelito has developed its own Intellectual Properties for solutions under the FinCraftTM brand in the areas of Transactions Management, Payments, Regulatory & Risk Reporting, Mobility, Data Management and BI & Analytics. The development is supported through industry best quality management processes.
