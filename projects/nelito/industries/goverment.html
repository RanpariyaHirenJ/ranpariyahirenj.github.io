E Governance Software Solution

The effective use of IT services in government administration can greatly enhance existing efficiencies, bring down communication costs, and increase transparency in the functioning of various departments. Technology optimizes the delivery of government services as it caters to a large base of people across different segments and geographical locations. The government benefits from reduced duplication of work. In addition, the processes of data collection, analysis and audit are simplified, and become less tedious.

At Nelito, we have proven domain expertise in e-Governance. Nelito's e-Gov Software Solution utilizes our experience in large scale e-Governance projects and brings value to our customers with our domain experience and blend of management and technical expertise. It is an integrated model developed for national and state governments to align their services with the changing needs of a developing economy.

Our digital government solutions use technologies such as, enterprise mobility, analytics and cloud computing to help organizations improve performance and develop forward-thinking, citizen-centric programs.

Key e-Governance Projects

Director General of Assam Rifles (DGAR)

  • Complete end-to-end Automation of personnel records based on Service Rules covering 1.2 lakhs serving & retired personnel.
  • Covers process right from Recruitment Rally to Post retirement.
  • Implemented across 101 units/locations spread across North East India.

National Rural Health Mission, Maharashtra – ASHA Application

  • Web-based Application Software for Data Capture and Management of ASHA (Health) Workers Information, Work performed, Payments and Training in entire state.
  • System managed more than 60,000 Asha's across the state.
  • Can be extended to similar field level functionaries for various government departments.

Jharkhand State Housing Board (JSHB), Jharkhand Government

  • Systems study, analysis, design, development and implementation of Integrated computerized system (ERP system).
  • Client-server based system with functionalities enabled for updating relevant data to the web based Application for Head office up to Divisional Offices across the state.

Drug Inventory System

  • Online system for inventory management of medicine and supplies from procurement to consumption by end user.

Grievance Redressal System

  • Employee Grievance System with workflow

Transfers Management System

  • Track and Perform the Transfer Activities of Staffs in various Health Institutions and Health Offices.

Mois – Medical Officers Information System

  • Web Application to capture Institutions details and Medical Officer's details of Various Staffs working in various Health Institutions and Health Offices.

Construction Tracker

  • Software application to track all the construction activities, their approval process and Fund Provisioning across all the divisions in the entire state.

Lok Shahir Annabhau Sathe Development Corporation, Social Justice & Special Assistance Department, Maharashtra

  • Web Application for end-to-end loan management system for financial assistance provided by Government for specialized purposes.

Public Works and Education Department, Maharashtra

  • Software application to track all the physical construction activities for building schools, sports facilities & hospitals.

Manipur E-District project for Citizen Services

  • As System Integrator to create a state-wide platform for citizen services like various type of registrations, certificates, ancillary services of Govt. to citizens.
  • Deployed across the entire state and covers all the departments.
