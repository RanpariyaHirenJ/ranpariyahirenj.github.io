Nelito Systems Ltd. was formed as a joint venture of, Nelco India (Tata Group) and ITOCHU Corporation Japan in 1995.

In March 2017, DTS Corporation Japan took significant minority shares (44%) in Nelito. In 2019, DTS Corporation wholly acquired Nelito Systems. Moving towards business synergy with Nelito in the banking and financial sector, DTS aims to enhance the capability of the DTS group in the financial sector and strengthen its global presence.

About Nelito

Nelito Systems Ltd., provides software products & services for Banking, Financial Services, Micro-Finance and Government verticals globally. Formed in the year 1995, Nelito has earned a unique reputation for its solutions and delivery Excellence with more than 400 customers in 20 countries across 4 continents. The company also provides IT service comprising of Application development and maintenance, consulting and IT outsourcing.

Our personalized software products and cost-effective implementations distinguish us, with our esteemed customers, industry peers, and independent research firms. Nelito combines its proactive approach, domain knowledge with a commitment to quality to deliver innovative solutions and services in an affordable and agile way. Over the last two decades, Nelito has built deep domain expertise and offers Excellence with Agility.

About DTS Corporation Japan

DTS Corporation Japan was established on August 25, 1972 and is headquartered in Hatchobori, Chuo-ku, Tokyo. For over 40 years, DTS has been engaging in Japanese Mega Bank's projects as a main consultant company. The DTS Group provides customers in Japan with added value through software solution-oriented business development and Business Process Outsourcing (BPO), as well as consulting, designing, developing, building infrastructure and constructing information systems. DTS and Nelito are dedicated to financial sector and have lots of domain expertise in banking systems such as loans, deposits, payments, trade finance and CMS.

The DTS Group has 13 companies in total: 8 Japanese companies and 5 companies outside Japan. Overseas bases are located in six cities: DTS America (New York, Los Angeles), DTS Shanghai (Shanghai), DLSE (Dalian), DTS Vietnam (Hanoi), and Nelito Systems Ltd (Mumbai). DTS supports customers in a wide range of business areas.

To know more about DTS kindly visit the following link - https://www.dts.co.jp/corp/company/
