Sanjay Dube | Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Sanjay Dube, prior to joining Nelito looked after the Fuel, Logistics and Land vertical for The Tata Power Company Limited. Prior to that, he looked after Strategy and Performance Analysis and before that, he looked after Treasury, Investor Relations and Corporate Finance, overseeing the Treasury operations of the TATA Power Group of companies and has been involved in several significant transactions during this stint. He has served on the Board of Directors on a number of companies in the TATA Power and Tata Group. He brings with him over 32 years of experience, of which, over 24 years were spent in the Tata Group, starting with Tata Sons Limited, the parent company.

Bhushan Samant | Head - Finance & Commercial

Bhushan Samant has over 25 years of experience in finance, accounting & planning and has performed diverse financial roles in leadership position in the Company over the last 20 years. He is currently the Head – Finance and Commercials of the company. He holds a MBA in finance and has done his bachelors in Commerce.

With strong accounting and financial management fundamentals, he has successfully handled various aspects of the finance function during his career. His contribution in the areas of MIS and Corporate Taxation in particular, has been commendable. He also looks after statutory compliance, corporate governance and all financial audit related activities.

Ashish Varun | Head - International Sales, Marketing & Pre Sales

Ashish Varun is responsible for expanding Nelito's footprint in international markets and also leads Nelito's marketing activities globally. Earlier, He led sales for Nelito's Government and Banking business in West and North India.

Prior to Nelito, he has worked in Public Sector, Quasi Public Sector & Private Sector organizations in senior roles encompassing Pre-Sales, Sales, Marketing, Finance, IT Consulting and business management. He brings with him a blend of techno-commercial experience and a track-record of building & managing business units from incubation stage across markets & solutions.

Deeply passionate about Skill Development in India with technology, Ashish is an executive member of various Committees like 1) Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (Govt. of India) appointed IMC committee and 2) Maharashtra State Committee on Skill Development & Affirmative Action appointed by Confederation of Indian Industry (CII).

Ashish holds an MBA (IT & Finance) from Department of Management Studies, IIT-Roorkee and BIS from Hans Raj College, University of Delhi.

Viraf Sirvala | Head - Sales (India)

Viraf has over 28 years of rich and varied experience in sales and marketing. In the BFSI domain, he has successfully spearheaded large value Sales deals for Core Banking, Core Insurance, Risk Management, Payment Switch, ATMs, CTS, ADF, Treasury, Financial Inclusion and many more.

He has worked with reputed groups such as Forbes, Wadia, and HCL. In between, Viraf has had a successful entrepreneurial venture in the IT domain, wherein he was the cofounder of an IT company, which he brought it up and eventually profitably sold to a US business group.

Viraf holds a BE degree in Electronics and Telecommunication.

Rakesh Verma | Head – India Sales (East)

Rakesh is responsible for Company's business in East, Managing operations and Relationship building with major focused clients such as PSU Banks, RRBs, UCBs, PACs, MFIs, NBFCs, Small Banks, Tata Companies, Some focused Govt Customers etc.

He joined Nelco (transferred to Nelito later) in January 1987 and has 28 plus years of experience in the software industry. One of his assignment included being sent to USA for a period of two years for setting up Nelito's wholly owned subsidiary, for Core Banking Solution and QA processes at Sanchez Inc (Malvern, PA).

He has earlier worked with Rohtas Industries Limited (Times of India Group), Texmaco Limited (Birla Group).

Sharath Nair | Head – India Sales (South)

Sharath Nair was appointed as Regional Business Head of Nelito in January 2017. He is currently responsible for Nelito's market engagements in Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. He has been closely involved in setting up and growing the company business across market segments.

Started his professional career in 1997, Sharath has an industry experience of several years in Banking and Financial Services Technology for building and executing business strategy through strong key account management, sales and delivery. Prior to joining Nelito, he was running own business ventures. Sharath also worked for various organisations, and major part his career spend in strategic business development and customer service in South Asia region.

Sharath is a Post Graduate Diploma holder in Computer Application from IHRD (Institute of Human Resources Development) Kerala and Bachelor of Science graduate from University of Kerala.

Gopal Dhuri | AVP - Delivery

Gopal Dhuri has over 26 years of rich experience in Banking and Banking Information Technology Industry. He heads Delivery for FinCraft and Other surrounding solutions along with customer support and services after implementation.

Being a banker he has rich experience in Strategic Planning, Banking software Implementation, Software Product Development, Program Management, Delivery Management, Functional Consultancy, Key Account Management in various organizations viz. HP, Zenith Computers, NextStep Infotech.

He holds Master Degree from Mumbai University in Advance Accounting.

Umashankar Kanchi | Head – SWIFT Business

Umashankar heads Nelito's SWIFT business line which has a leadership position in the region. He is a 2-decade-plus experienced SWIFT-professional. An engineer by qualification, he started his career with the Central Government of India and subsequently worked as a banker.

He leads teams responsible for SWIFT Sales, Project implementations & Integration, Service Bureau and Support.

Dhriti Singh | Head – Services

Dhriti Singh is the head of Professional Consulting Services (Manpower) & BPO Division of Nelito. He has over 15+ years of experience into BPO/IT, ITes services. His key role has been into managing Profit Center Management, Operations & Service Delivery and Client Portfolio.
He has worked on various processes/programs such as Telecom, Mobile payment gateway, Social media platform, Google Micro work & ISPs. He has managed/supported customers across globe from USA/UK/Africa & UAE.

He is a graduate in Economics & MBA into Operations & Productions Management.

Ajay Chadha | Executive Director & Head of Global Sales

Ajay Chadha has vast experience in International Sales, especially with Japanese companies across the globe. He has been working with Japanese companies spanning various sectors for nearly three decades. He holds diversified expertise in the Automobile and IT Industries.

His recent professional experience includes work as Area Sales Director at NEC, where he was responsible for sales and business expansion across Japan, India and Singapore.

Ajay Chadha is a graduate in Commerce from University of Delhi and also holds Post Intensive Advance Diploma in Japanese Language from University of Delhi.

In addition, he has Diploma in International Marketing from Symbosis, Pune. He was awarded for being the State level topper in the JLPT LEVEL 1. He also secured all India 3rd position in the Senior level Japanese Language Essay Contest and has won Japanese Language Speech Contest (North India).
