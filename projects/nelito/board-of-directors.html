Minoru Takeuchi | Chairman

Minoru Takeuchi is currently holding the position of Executive director in DTS Corporation. He is also heading DTS Financial business division.

He is the Director of Data Links and Director of DTS KYUSHU (Subsidiary of DTS). He was the president of DTS America till March 2017.

Minoru Takeuchi has a vast experience in Financial Institutional domain. Minoru Takeuchi's expertise in Financial domain helped DTS Financial division business grow and one-third of DTS turnover is from BFSI sector.

Minoru Takeuchi graduated from Department of Technology, Tokyo Denki University in 1985.

Ajay Chadha | Executive Director

Ajay Chadha has vast experience in International Sales, especially with Japanese companies across the globe. He has been working with Japanese companies spanning various sectors for nearly three decades. He holds diversified expertise in the Automobile and IT Industries.

His recent professional experience includes work as Area Sales Director at NEC, where he was responsible for sales and business expansion across Japan, India and Singapore.

Ajay Chadha is a graduate in Commerce from University of Delhi and also holds Post Intensive Advance Diploma in Japanese Language from University of Delhi.

In addition, he has Diploma in International Marketing from Symbosis, Pune. He was awarded for being the State level topper in the JLPT LEVEL 1. He also secured all India 3rd position in the Senior level Japanese Language Essay Contest and has won Japanese Language Speech Contest (North India).

Sanjay Dube | Director

Mr. Sanjay Dube, prior to joining Nelito looked after the Fuel, Logistics and Land vertical for The Tata Power Company Limited. Prior to that, he looked after Strategy and Performance Analysis and before that, he looked after Treasury, Investor Relations and Corporate Finance, overseeing the Treasury operations of the TATA Power Group of companies and has been involved in several significant transactions during this stint. He has served on the Board of Directors on a number of companies in the TATA Power and Tata Group. He brings with him over 32 years of experience, of which, over 24 years were spent in the Tata Group, starting with Tata Sons Limited, the parent company.
