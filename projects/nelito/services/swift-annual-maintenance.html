Nelito is accredited to implement the Alliance and SWIFTTM Net products such as Alliance Access, Alliance Entry, Alliance Gateway, SWIFTTM Net Link, etc. Besides, Nelito is well-positioned to offer technical assistance locally or remotely on SWIFTTM Net connectivity or Centralization of foreign branches / offices.

This service envisages onsite or remote interventions of our technical experts to accomplish patch updations or problem resolution during times of emergencies arising out of issues related to application database or other configuration errors which could make critical features/components of application non-usable.

SWIFTTM Annual Maintenance service pack provides professional SWIFTTM services remotely either by way of mails, chat/IM, phones and/or securely accessing the systems remotely for trouble shooting. Wherever remote access is possible customers provide Nelito's Engineers to secure access to SWIFTTM Server remotely. Nelito would suggest the licensed remote access software to use by the customer for remote access.
