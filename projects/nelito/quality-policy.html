Company Quality Policy

  • Home
  • Quality

Nelito professionals maintain consistent high quality level in whatever they do. Quality Assurance both at product and process levels is given a key importance at Nelito.

This policy applies to all aspects of business life of the Company including human resources, corporate communication, finance, and administration.

The professionals of the Company are made aware of this policy, and are directed to put in their concerted effort towards ensuring that Nelito is regarded as a Company offering the highest possible quality of products and services to its customers.

In addition to the Nelito Quality Policy, all software professionals and training professionals are also guided by the process area-wise Quality Policies.
