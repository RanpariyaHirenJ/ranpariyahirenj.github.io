The Nelito team is unified by a common vision.

The key to our success has always been our people, who are highly motivated with a zest for life that is reflected in all they do. In turn the organization has provided them with abundant opportunities for growth. At Nelito, careers are driven solely on the basis of merit. Rewards and recognition are based on an objective evaluation of the employee’s performance, potential, criticality and market value.

We believe that freedom inspires ideas and therefore facilitate a work environment that nurtures excellence. We leverage not only on individual performances but depend extensively upon Teamwork and Group cohesiveness.

We seek people who are aligned with our vision and are committed to make it a reality. An ideal candidate would possess a mix of technical and analytical skills, adaptability, willingness to learn and an open mind. If you find a fitment with the above description, a highly professional environment awaits you. You can create value for yourself by creating value for the organization.
