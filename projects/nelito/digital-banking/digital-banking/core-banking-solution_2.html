Overview - FincraftTM Core Banking Solution

Transformation in core banking today is faster than ever. Technologies like blockchain, artificial intelligence, mobility, big data are evolving rapidly and this is disrupting the prevalent business models of banks. Today's customers are trailblazers and are adopting these recent technologies which has changed the way they interact with their banks.

Financial Institutes are looking to fill this gap between customer expectations and the way conventional banks operate. How can your bank make things happen for its customers amidst this digital disruption? The secret to its success is the dual strategy of New and Renew with FinCraftTM Core Banking Solution.

FinCraftTM Core Banking Solution enables you to drive innovation and growth, provide seamless customer experience and achieve operational excellence.

Our CBS is a comprehensive core banking solution for banks built on advanced web technologies. Its platform-independent, open-system architecture and cutting-edge technology enables smooth integration with existing and future systems of financial institutions. It helps automate front-end and back-end processes of banks to achieve centralized and smooth processing.

FinCraftTM CBS solution suite combines multiple functionalities:

  • Design and development of core banking software
  • Testing and test automation
  • Application enhancement and maintenance support

Key Features

  • Omnichannel
  • Straight through processing
  • Complete front and back office solution
  • Provision of Login security, Module wise rights, Account rights, System locks
  • Banking finance including general ledger and reporting
  • Customer-centric Architecture, Real-time 360-degree view of customer relationships
  • Agile Solution, highly extensible through parameterization and user driven customization

Benefits

  • Real-time, complete customer information
  • Product builder
  • Infinite scalability
  • Extremely low TCO
  • Zero risk of obsolescence
  • Anytime anywhere banking through multiple channels
