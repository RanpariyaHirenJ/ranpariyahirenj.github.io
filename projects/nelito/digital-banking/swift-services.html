  • SWIFT Services

SWIFT Competency of Nelito is built on the strengths of Nelito's market penetration, experience and strong presence across the financial markets in the Indian sub-continent (IN & SC). The engagement with SWIFT is uniquely poised to offer complete package of onboarding, implementation, support of various SWIFT interfaces, products, solutions, connectivity as well as meet other allied integration requirements. Nelito is the largest SWIFT Services' Provider of the region, having more than 100 institutions as highly satisfied customers not only in the IN & SC region but from other APAC, Europe and US regions. Nelito supports full range of SWIFT products & services and on all qualified platforms. Our Customers include Financial regulators, Public Sector, Private Sector, Foreign and Co-operative banks besides other financial institutions, vendors, System Integrators and Corporates. In fact, any and every of our customer is a reference customer.

Nelito has vast experience not only in the traditional Alliance portfolio products but also the new age add-ons and offerings like IPLA (Integration Platform), Sanction's Screening and the like. Besides, Nelito is well-positioned to offer technical assistance locally or remotely on SWIFT Net connectivity or Centralization of foreign offices. SWIFT & Nelito are jointly offering local support and NG-RTGS deployments.
