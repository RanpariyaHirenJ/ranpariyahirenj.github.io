  • Third Party CBS Implementation

Nelito has a vast experience in successfully implementing Third Party CBS like Finacle Core Banking Solution for different sized banks.

Nelito started its Finacle journey in the year 2004 by becoming partner to Infosys Technologies and Hewlett-Packard. The Finacle practice was started with barely 10 resources in February 2004. The number of resources is over 300 by the year 2004, 450 by the year 2005 and over 600 by the year 2006. Nelito has set up an Excellence Centre with Finacle 7x and 10x infrastructures at its Head Office and has been able to create talents in-house.

It has set Industry record in migrating over 150 branches every week for various Banks. This effort warranted requirement of 90 Data Migration professionals and 450+ support personnel at branches. The PAN India presence of Nelito permitted migration of branches at such a high magnitude. We have a project experience of rolling out more than 17,000 Bank Branches and have imparted knowledge enhancement services to over 25,000 Bank users. We also offer Finacle e-Banking (FEBA) solutions.
