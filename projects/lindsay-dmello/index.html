ME

Starting as a drummer at an early age, I've been on a long journey through music where I've been fortunate to have had influences of various musical styles. Thanks to my family, friends, fellow musicians and industry peers, the last 20 years of my journey have taken me through rock, pop, jazz, funk, drum n,bass, electronica and Bollywood.

My fascination for drumming began at an early age when my parents took me to an Elvis Presley concert screening at Sterling theater in Mumbai. With the acquisition of a drum kit in the early 90's, my passion for drumming and music has only kept growing.
