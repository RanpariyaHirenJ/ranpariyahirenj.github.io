The Refreshing Research canpaign is an initiative by Organisation of Pharmaceutical Producers of India (OPPI). As an organisation we believe that reserach is at the crux of new inventions and innovations for a better tommorow.

Why we decided to take up this campaign?

The fact is everything we love and find interesting, is built by scientist and researchers who dedicate themselves to unravelling the mysteries of the world, behind closed doors. Science is often seen as ‘not cool.’

Our goal is to engage younger minds with Science and Research, showing them that research is exciting, engaging and Science is ‘cool’.

We are looking for innovations in the following areas:

  • Women's Health
  • Public Health - Anti Microbial Resistance & Vaccines
  • Supply chain management
  • Sustainability in pharma industry
