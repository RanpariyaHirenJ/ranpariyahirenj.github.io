FAQs

1) What is the contest about?

Refreshing Research campaign is an initiative by Organisation of Pharmaceutical Producers of India (OPPI) whose objective is to engage younger minds with Science and Research, thus enabling them to opt for it as a career path.

2) Can there be an individual participation or it’s only a group contest?

No individual participation will be considered. A team of 5 students along with 1

3) Are there any age restrictions for participating in the contest?

Yes. Students between age 18 to 21 can participate in the contest

4) Are there any necessary documents which are required to participate in the contest?

Yes. You’ll need your college ID card & a letter from your college dean verifying your eligibility to participate in the contest.

5) What is the entry registration deadline?

The entry registration deadline is __ October, 2019

6) How many days will the registrations be open?

Registrations will be for a week after the announcement date

7) Who will be judging the contest?

An online panel of judges consisting of eminent scientists and leaders in the pharma industry, along with broader participationfrom the student community, who can publicly vote for their favourites, will award scores to participants

8) What will the winning team receive?

The winning team will receive lab coats designed by renowned artist & other gratifications.
