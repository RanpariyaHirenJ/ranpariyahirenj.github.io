FAQs

  • What is a Topic

    A Topic is a set of search parameters based on which you will see results from Blogs, Forums, Consumer Forums, Review Sites, News sites, Twitter, Facebook and other social channels. A Topic can have unlimited set of keywords

  • How long is the data retained in the tool

    Lifetime.

  • Do you offer Discounts?

    Yes we do offer discounts. 10% discount on yearly plans and 20% on bi-annual billing (2 year commitment)

  • Are there any features that are not available in the lesser plans?

    No. All features are enabled in all plans including trial account

  • What is a Social Profile

    Social Profile is Brand or Competitor social profile. Eg, Facebook page, Twitter handle, Instagram page, YouTube channel. You can analyze various metrics such as engagement, Impressions, Influencers for Brand and competition.

  • Can I cancel my subscription later?
