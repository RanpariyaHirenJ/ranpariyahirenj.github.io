Simplifies your need to publish content on multiple networks

Konnect Insights’s social publishing module simplifies your need to publish content on multiple social media networks. You can simultaneously post from all your social profiles in one go. The social networks currently supported by Konnect Social for publishing are Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn.

Social Publishing.

  • Features

    • Create as many campaigns as your social media campaign demands
    • Compose messages as per the character limit of each social network
    • Attach images to scheduled messages
    • Easily map all necessary social media profiles in the tool for publishing through them
    • Select the frequency of publishing if any (E.g. – Daily, weekly & monthly)
    • Use the calendar interface for scheduling multiple posts throughout the year
    • Edit scheduled messages or delete them as and when required
