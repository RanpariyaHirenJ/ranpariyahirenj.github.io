Pool – Beach – Pier – We have it all here.

The Colonial Inn is open year round and shows its old world charm as it proudly stands as one of the oldest motels on the beach in Nags Head, North Carolina. Located next door to the Nags Head Fishing Pier and within walking distance to several restaurants in the area, guests can enjoy fishing, dining and sunbathing at the beach or by the outdoor pool.
