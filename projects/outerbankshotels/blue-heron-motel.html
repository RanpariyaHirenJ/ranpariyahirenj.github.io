Highly rated Oceanfront Nags Head Motel

When nothing but the best matters to you, the Blue Heron Motel brings you that complete feeling of having experienced the best. Experience fantastic ocean views from every room. Yes, that's right! All 30 rooms offer magnificient unobstructed views of the Atlantic Ocean. And, to make things even sweeter, enjoy the delights of 2 outdoor swimming pools right by the ocean and the added luxury of an indoor heated pool and hot tub for those unexpected rainy and cold days.

This pet friendly hotel offers old world charm with a touch of southern hospitality. A place you can truly call home away from home. Relax on your private balcony and enjoy the beautiful ocean views. Within a 10 minute walk are popular restaurants like Miller's Waterfront, Owen's, Sam & Omies & Scammell's Surf Shop.

Nearby tourist attractions are Jennette's Pier, Mutiny Bay Adventure Golf, The Outlets, The USS Heron wreck and more.

Q&A's

How far is the hotel from the beach?

The Blue Heron Motel is 200 ft from the beach. Most guests mentioned a walking distance of 1 minute. To get to the beach, simply walk from the hotel's private access road a few steps to get to the beach.

Are there restaurants nearby?

This hotel has a great walk-score for dining. Within a 5 minute walk is the Dunes Restaurant, Millers Waterfront Restaurant, Owen's Restaurant and Sam and Omie's. A short 5 minute drive can get you to Tale of the Whale, Basnight's Lone Cedar Cafe and more amazing seafood restaurants and bars.

Is the hotel near Jennette's Pier

Yes. The Blue Heron Motel is 0.7 miles away from the Jennete's Pier. This is a 10 minute walk.
