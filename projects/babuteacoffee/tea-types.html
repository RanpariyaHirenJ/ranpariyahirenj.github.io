White Tea/ Chai Called White, seemingly pale yellow

White Tea is tea made of young tea leaves that are minimally processed. White Tea is not rolled or oxidised, resulting in a flavour characterised as “lighter” than Green or traditional Black Teas. Though called White Tea – brewing the leaves gives you a pale yellow decoction.

Assam Tea The ideal Indian Breakfast Tea

Assam Chai is a full-bodied brew that is brisk, strong and bright-hued. It is grown in the North East Indian state of Assam. The state of Assam is the largest tea-growing region in the world and the plantations are all at sea level. The tea is favoured by those seeking a strongly flavoured drink that is malty. It is a great way to prepare you to face the day, at breakfast time, though it can be consumed with equal impact any time of the day.

Goan Chai A proprietary blend from our Master Tea Blenders

Goan Chai is our proprietary blend unique in its flavouring. It is uniquely Goan and carries with it a part of the Susegad culture. Susegad is a word derived from the Portuguese word – Sossegado meaning “quiet”. In Goa though, it has come to mean the languid, relaxed and laid back attitude that has made it one of the world’s favourite holiday destinations. The Chai is refreshingly fresh and has the effect of calming you and bringing you back into the “Goa” frame of mind.
