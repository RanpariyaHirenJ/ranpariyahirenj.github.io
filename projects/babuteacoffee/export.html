Price Ranges

Once you are satisfied with the quality of our blends and the prices, you have to place an Export Order. Our C&F agents complete the required paperwork & clearances required from the Indian government in record time.

Grades of the Tea available for export

OP Orange Pekoe is the main grade, the quality of which is in slotted between OP1 and OPA and consists of long wiry leaf without tips

OP1 It's a whole leaf grade, slightly delicate than OP, with long, wiry leaf and light liquor

FOP Flowery Orange Pekoe is a high quality tea with a long leaf and few tips, considered the second grade in Assam and with dark liquor

BOP Broken Orange Pekoe is a broken leaf grade that we procure both from Assam & South India

BP Broken Pekoe is the most common variety available in India to be used in tea bags

Dust We blend Pekoe Dust 1 with other grades to create some signature teas

Fanning Orange Fannings, Pekoe Fannings and Broken Orange Pekoe Fannings grades are availabe with us
