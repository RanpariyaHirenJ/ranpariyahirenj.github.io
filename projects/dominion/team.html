John D. Saunders | General Manager

John brings to Dominion over 42 years of parts experience, beginning with G.G. Moss Co. in the '70's. Following the sale of his own very successful auto parts warehousing distributorship, John was Manager of the Richmond and Tidewater areas for AutoPart International.

John has three children and seven grandchildren, with the girls ahead by one! He loves to get in a round of golf when work allows and enjoys travelling, music, reading, sports and (drumroll!) cooking!

Contact John to "cook up" a great deal on your next parts order!

Michael W. Larkin | Mopar and Fleet Sales

Michael has an extensive background in residential mortgages and made the transition to auto parts approximately 4 years ago. Michael specializes in parts needs of corporate and fleet clients and is totally committed to their success. As validation to his sense of devotion, he and his future wife met in the 7th grade and have been together ever since!

When not supporting the needs of his wife and clients, Michael loves to get in a round of golf and a few hours of snow skiing.

John K. Faison | VP/General Manager

John is a 40-year veteran of The Dominion Auto Group and the automobile industry. Prior to his appointment in 1985 as Parts Director for all GM franchises, John served as Parts Manager for three of Dominions dealerships. His knowledge of parts warehousing, logistics and marketing has led Dominion to the #1 volume GM parts operation in Central Virginia.

John enjoys spending time with his daughter and grandson. He is fond of sports, and likes to relax by hunting or fishing with friends.

Gary R. Hahn | Warehouse Manager

Outside of work, Gary enjoys sports, movies, camping and fishing. He adores his pets, and refers to them as his four-legged kids with a tail.
