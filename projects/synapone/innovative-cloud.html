Innovative Cloud Solutions

SynapOne empowers enterprises in their continuous transformation journey by providing full stack cloud services. SynapOne’s unique ‘Business First’ strategy and industrialized solutions approach supported by strong industry domain and partner ecosystem positions us uniquely to help the enterprises of tomorrow to drive business acceleration, customer experience and connected insights.

Cloud Strategy and Consulting

We help drive cost savings, flexibility, security and compliance for mission critical applications. Stemming from our own cloud-based environment with prebuilt cloud solution components and those of our cloud partners, we will help you choose the best cloud technologies and map out a strategy for your business that will:

Virtualize processes

Accelerate time-to-market and increase profitability

Improve the customer experience

Enhance utilization of your IT infrastructure

Increase workforce collaboration

Cloud Services

We can help you transform your applications by hosting, extending or building them on IaaS or PaaS cloud platforms. Synaptein’s Cloud Application Services help you move to the cloud seamlessly, whether you’re migrating an off-the-shelf package or developing a new application from scratch. Once cloud-enabled, you can easily scale web applications, set up testing environments, experiment with high-performance computing (HPC) applications and more.
