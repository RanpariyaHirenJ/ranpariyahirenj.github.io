Unprecedented times calls for some extraordinary measures. The 21-day lockdown underway across India due to the dreaded Coronavirus outbreak has led to citizens grappling with accessing several services.

CMS Info Systems, India's largest cash and payments solutions company, is offering 'CMS Cash2Home', an innovative and free doorstep cash delivery service to help citizens get cash safely at home without needing to step out.

CMS will support Banks to provide Cash Delivery Services, to Senior Citizens and Differently-Abled free of cost. 'CMS Cash2Home' will help citizens get cash to pay bills, access monthly salaries and clear dues. More importantly, it also enables contactless access to cash for senior citizens who are most vulnerable in today's threat of a community spread of the virus.
