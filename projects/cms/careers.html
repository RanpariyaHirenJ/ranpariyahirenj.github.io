When keeping the country's circulation system running, there is no downtime. No. Matter. What.

CMS team prides itself on running a tight operation that can work in the toughest situations, delivering on the highest standards of operations. Our work takes us to over 98% plus of the districts in India, every single day. When disaster struck at Uttaranchal, Chennai and Srinagar, we were among the first to hit the roads, along with emergency services. Our teams worked double time during Demonetisation, be it for evacuating old currency, recalibrating ATMs or replenishing them as soon as we received money from the banks. With over 282 offices spread across the entire breadth of the country, ours' is a diverse, cross cultural group that gives immense opportunity to anyone who wishes to cut their teeth at one of the most complex and secure ATM management companies in India.
