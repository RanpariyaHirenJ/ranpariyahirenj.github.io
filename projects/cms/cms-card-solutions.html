Card Personalisation Software

Our proprietary card personalization management software 'CPMS Gnxt Software' developed in-house is used by almost all banks issuing high volumes of Credit and Debit cards.

CPMS GNxT is the ultimate tool for controlling, coordinating and managing card personalization activities.

Card Personalisation

The personalization aspect essentially entails the printing, embossing and indenting of variable data in line with the card mailer, along with automatic affixing and collation, which by the way, varies from card to card. We offer complete personalisation services from our Card Centre at Vashi, New Mumbai. Facilities, vetted and certified by Visa, Mastercard and Rupay.

Our Card Centres can specifically handle

  • EMV/Smart Card Personalization - Contact & Contactless
  • Magnetic Encoding
  • Embossing & Tipping
  • Front & Rear Indenting
  • Colour Photo printing
  • Graphics & text printing
  • Full Card Image Printing
  • Signature Printing
  • Laser Engraving

Card Fulfilment Services

Rounding off the offering is our fulfilment services which delivers the cards to their end users. Accompanied by relevant, personalized messages, it takes care of the little final details. The project is completed with

  • Procurement of card carrier, envelope, stationery etc.
  • Card carrier personalization, card affixing to card carrier, insertion into correctly addressed envelopes
  • Handover of ready packets to the bank's or customer's designated courier or postal service.

WHAT IS EMV?

EMV is a de facto standard for debit and credit bank cards, jointly developed by Europay, MasterCard and Visa. EMV will mean that the current magnetic stripe banking cards will be replaced with smart cards in the coming years. This change is a major one, considering there exist about 1.5 billion international debit and credit cards, and the available points of usage are in the range of 20 million. Learn More
