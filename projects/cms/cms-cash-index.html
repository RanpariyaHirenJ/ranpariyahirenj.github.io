CMS Cash Index

Want to know how well a country is doing? Follow the money.

Every year the CMS network moves a little more than 7,00,000 crores of currency through its systems. Through the 57000+ ATMs that we replenish and the 35000+ retail and enterprise chains whose cash payments we collect, process and bank every single day. The CMS Cash Index is series of cuts of this movement and gives a fascinating glimpse of the rhythm of commerce in our country.
