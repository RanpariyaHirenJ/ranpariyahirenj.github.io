About Slonkit

You are getting ready to go to office and your child comes to you asking for some money. You can't just say a NO. You forget to keep a tab on this ad-hoc expenditure and also have no control or clue on where the child spends the money. If you are one such parent, Slonkit is for you.

You want to keep an account of what's happening to the pocket money given to your child? One might expect that giving pocket money might be accompanied with a message about the value of saving and even some guidelines on savings. The parents would be keen on playing an important role in inculcating the best values and the drive for financial smartness, besides ensuring their children's safety.

We look at leveraging this need prevalent in both the young adults (kids,teens,adults) and their parents by creating a platform that:

  • Brings in transparency on the spending of the pocket money given to the child
  • Creates an element of trust between the parents and the youngsters
  • Trains them through insightful information, for financial astuteness

Based on these parameters, we have launched SLONKIT - "state-of-the-art smartphone wallet"

It's the smart solution to enable 12-24 year olds to gain confidence with money, learning under your guidance. And it makes your life as a parent just that little bit easier. You set the ground rules and give your children the independence to manage their own money through Slonkit. They'll soon grow in confidence, developing good money habits for life. And they'll love the freedom and responsibility Slonkit brings.
