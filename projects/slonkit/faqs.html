Faq's

Getting Started
  • What is Slonkit? Slonkit Card is a reloadable prepaid gift card  for your child. It allows kids to shop in-store or online with the set amount of money that parents have loaded on the Slonkit card for the child.
  • How does Slonkit work? Slonkit is developed to provide a secure mechanism for children to shop online/in-store wherein the parent can load money to the child's Slonkit wallet, control and monitor his/her spendings.
  • How do I create a Slonkit Wallet? The parent will have to download the Slonkit app at Google Playstore/Appstore on his/her mobile and register. Alternatively, he can also sign up on the website and create a Slonkit account using his/her login credentials. Once the parent creates his/her child's wallet, the child can then download the app and get started with Slonkit
Parents
  • How long will it take to load money into my wallet? Immediately as it's a real time transfer. You can transfer money from credit cards, debit cards and net banking.
  • How will I know my child's spending pattern? Slonkit dashboard will provide you with graphs/charts about your child's spending pattern. For eg: a graph portraying the daily spent with respect to the different pre-defined categories of his/her child.
  • What are the advantages of giving my child a Slonkit card? * Can structure the allowance given to your child
    * Can track his/her monthly/daily spending
    * Can teach him/her the essentials of saving and spending prudently
  • Can my child receive gifts through Slonkit? Yes, his relatives and friends can load money into his/her wallet
  • What if my child's phone/card gets stolen/lost? You can deactivate the Slonkit app of your child or call the customer care.
  • Can I control my child's spendings? Yes, you can set controls on your child's spendings across predefined categories such as Food and Beverages, Mobile Recharge, Books and Stationary, Hypermarkets, Retail etc.
  • Can I check the balance on my child's Slonkit account? Yes, you can check the balance on your child's Slonkit account.
  • Can I get alerts when my child's slonkit account balance goes low? Yes
  • What if my child has not spent his/her allowance for the month? It would get carried forward to the next month. Or your child can transfer his saved allowance to any of the banks we have tied up with.

Smart Shopper ( Kids,Teens and Adults )
  • Why should I use Slonkit? Everytime you got to shop online/in-store, you need to ask your parents for money/to purchase on your behalf. You can get rid of this daily hassle by using Slonkit. Your parents would be loading money into your Slonkit wallet every month and you can plan and shop accordingly.
  • What will be my monthly allowance? It will be equal to your monthly pocket money which your parent will be loading onto your Slonkit wallet.
  • How do I use Slonkit? Just download the Slonkit App on your mobile. You can start using it to shop online/in store in the predefined categories such as Food and Beverages, Books, Retail, Online Recharge etc. You can check your balance and transaction updates everytime you swipe your Slonkit card. You can set SMS alerts for every transaction on your Slonkit wallet.
  • How do I know which merchants/retailers accept Slonkit? It's pretty simple. Go to www.slonkit.com. Under 'Merchants Corner' section, you can view the list of merchants/retailers that accept SLONKIT.
  • Can I use my Slonkit mobile wallet/card anywhere in India? Yes it can be used anywhere in India.
  • Can I use the money as I want to spend? Yes based on the amount loaded on your Slonkit wallet.
  • What if my money got over? The card can be reloaded anytime and the balance will be reflected in an hours time.
  • Can I transfer money to my friend? Yes you can transfer money to your friend using the peer to peer transfer option.
  • I have forgotten my e-PIN? What should I do? Go to the website/wallet and reset your e-PIN password. Any available balance or pending orders will be restored in the new application. You are now good to go!
  • How can I ask for more money if I am exhausted with what I have? You can just send across a message to your parents or use the chat feature  on the app.
  • Can I check my balance everytime I make a transaction? Yes you can check your balance after every transaction. You can set SMS alerts  for each transaction that you make.
  • How to view my transaction summary? Steps? 
  • What if i don't utilize my monthly allowance allotted by my parents? It will get carried forward for 6 months post which your card will be disabled.
  • If I change my mobile phone but the cell number remains unchanged; does it mean I can still use SLONKIT? Yes, you can continue to use Slonkit as your Slonkit wallet is linked to your simcard and not the mobile handset. You just need to download the Slonkit app on your new handset.
