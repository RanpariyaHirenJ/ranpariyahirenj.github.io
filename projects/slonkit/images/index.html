    Map Elements

    Obsurfation makes finding and viewing conditions at your local beaches and waterways as simple as exploring a map. So what's on the map?

    • Locations

      Thousands of beach and waterway locations across Australia.

    • Swell Indicators

      Represents the swell forecast - displaying swell direction and size (feet).

    • Wind Indicators

      Represents the wind forecast - displaying wind direction and strength (knots).

    • Tide Indicators

      Represents the tide forecast - displaying tide phase, plus the rise and fall.
