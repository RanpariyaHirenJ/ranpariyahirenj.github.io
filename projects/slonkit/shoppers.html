Why Slonkit

Instead of asking for Monthly Allowance in cash or your using a Parent's add-on Debit Card, use Slonkit. It's so much easier and convenient- for both you and your parent. Slonkit is an App and Card payment method, where you own a Plastic Card and use it for shopping, travelling or eating out. The card is linked to the Slonkit App on Your and your Prent's mobile which shows up how much you are spending and on what.

  • First ask your parent to download the Slonkit App on his/her mobile and register.
  • Next he/she registers you as the user and downloads Slonkit on your mobile
  • Your parent now adds online the money you need, to your account
  • Your personalized Slonkit card is couriered to you and you can start using it at the partner merchant stores.
  • Every time you use, the spend shows up instantly on both your and your parent's mobile.
  • In case you run out of money, your parent can instantly top-up online. In case you want to make an out of limit spend, you can ask permission from your parent.
  • Introduce your friends to Slonkit and you can even transfer amongst yourself. Just like cash! Only much safer.
  • You are happy. Your parent is informed and relieved.
