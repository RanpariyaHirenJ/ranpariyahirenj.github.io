Parents Corner

Slonkit is a smartphone wallet for your kids. Be a modern parent and give your child the freedom to make his or her own purchase decisions, while you are virtually by their side, participating.

Help your kids become more responsible and mature with their spending. They will respect your trust and become conscious about financial planning, while thankful to you about the freedom you gifted them.

Slonkit is a Smartphone Based App + rechargeable Plastic Card which your kids can use across a multitude of Indian merchants of food, shopping, books, gadgets, travel etc. Some of them offer great deals to Slonkit users time to time which makes it very pocket friendly for the kids. The merchant list is growing thereby opening more doors of happiness for all.
