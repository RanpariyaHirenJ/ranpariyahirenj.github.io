Property is across the beach & Jennette's Pier

Located right across Nags Head's popular Jennette's Pier, the Seahorse Inn & Cottages offers travelers beautiful modest rooms and cottages. Enjoy scenic views of the Atlantic ocean from the Deluxe and King rooms. Families, in particular, appreciated the rustic cottages with efficiency kitchens. This hotel's minimalistic, yet adequate rooms, offer great price value compared to other hotels in the neighborhood. Sam & Omie's, the local fishermen's favorite seafood breakfast spot, is just a 1 minute walk away. Jennette's Pier is a 2 minute walk & popular attractions like the Cape Hatteras National Seashore & Whalebone Park are within a 5 minute walking distance. Customers rated this hotel highly for affordability, service and location.

#1 Hotel Location near Jennette's Pier

Previously the award winning Rodeway Inn, this Nags Head hotel changed ownership in early 2019, and was renamed the Seahorse Inn & Cottages. Since then, the Seahorse Inn & Cottages has continued the 5 star service tradition of it's predecessor, and has brought smiles to thousands of happy guests. This modest and unpretentious hotel offers affordable rates, simple rooms and great value for travelers.

Guests rated it the #1 location closest to Jennette's Pier. Families loved staying in the rustic no-frills cottages and other travelers enjoyed the ocean and inlet views from the Deluxe and King rooms. Hotel reviews also mentioned guests relaxing on the balcony chairs outside rooms and enjoying the idyllic views of Jennette's Pier.

This Nags Head hotel features standard rooms, efficiency rooms & cottages (w/kitchenettes). Standard rooms come equipped with convenience amenities like a microwave, mini-fridge & coffee maker. All rooms have LCD TV's, end tables and coffee tables with chairs. WiFi connectivity and speed was rated at 99% by 198 guests.

For fishing enthusiasts, Jennette's Pier is a 3 min walk. The highly popular Sam & Omie's seafood restaurant is 300 ft away. Need some groceries? Cahoon's Market is a 2 min walk. Looking for some great shopping deals? The top rated Outlets at Nags Head is a 9 min walk (0.5 mi). Top tourist attractions within a mile are Whalebone Park, First Flight Adventure Park & Cape Hatteras National Seashore. Within 5 miles is Nags Head Fishing Pier & Jockey's Ridge State Park.

Q&A's

Does the hotel have ocean view rooms?

Yes, a select few rooms (King Suite & Deluxe Double) have good ocean views. Other rooms have ocean views from the outdoor balcony. Guests enjoyed relaxing on the balcony chairs outside rooms. The balcony is a good place to enjoy great views of Jennette's Pier and the Atlantic ocean.

Do all rooms have kitchenette's?

Only the efficiency rooms and cottages have kitchenettes. All other standard rooms feature a mini-fridge and microwave for guest convenience.

Does the hotel have handicap accessible rooms?

Yes. This hotel has handicap accessible cottage efficiency rooms with 2 double beds and a kitchen. These rooms conform to the recommended ADA compliance standards.

Is this a pet-friendly hotel?

Yes. The Seahorse Inn & Cottages is a pet friendly property. There is a nominal daily fee for accompanying pets. Please note that this property has limited pet friendly rooms. Guests traveling with pets should confirm availability before arrival.
