Great value Buxton NC Hotel

Peace and tranquility beckon you to Hatteras Island Inn. Nestled in the small town of Buxton, NC, this unassuming hotel is a favorite among families, couples and solo adventure seekers. Guests loved the peaceful surroundings, proximity to the beach, sparkling clean rooms, great bedding and superb observation tower. Almost 60% of guests who stayed here, returned the following year, which is testimony to this hotel's exceptional southern charm and hospitality.

The Hatteras Island Inn offers 60 comfortable smoke-free rooms. Guests can choose between single King bed rooms, 2 double beds and a King bed room with pullout sofa. All rooms feature large LCD TV's, free high speed WiFi, microwave, mini-fridge, iron/ironing board, coffee maker and alarm clocks. For added convenience, guests can avail of on-site laundry service, outdoor grills and vending machines.

Popular local restaurants are within easy walking distance. The Diamond Shoals restaurant, Buxton Munch Company, Orange Blossom Bakery and Rusty's Surf & Turf are within walking distance. Top tourist attractions like the Cape Hatteras Lighthouse and Cape Hatteras National Seashore are a 10 min drive away. Guests rated this hotel a 9 for location and room amenities.

Q&A's

How far is the hotel from the beach?

The Hatteras Island Inn is 600 ft from the beach. Most guests mentioned a walking distance of 3-4 minutes. To get to the beach, walk across the pool to Oramar Dr. The beach is a 3 minute walk along Oramar Dr.

Are there restaurants nearby?

This hotel has a great walk-score for dining. Within a 5 minute walk is the Diamond Shoals Restaurant, Uncle Eddy's Homemade Custard, Dillards Corner Coffee Bar, Super Wings & Angelos Pizza. The Beach House Grill, Rusty's Surf & Turf, Lighthouse Sports Bar, The Buxton Much Co and Orange Blossom Bakery are a 10-15 min walk.

Is the hotel near Cape Hatteras Lighthouse?

Yes. The Hatteras Island Inn is 1.4 miles away from the Cape Hatteras Lighthouse. The historic Cape Hatteras National Seashore in just 2.2 miles away. Guests to this hotel also visited Lighthouse Horse rides (2.2 mi) & the Outer Banks Futuro House (5.5 mi)

Do this hotel have laundry facilities?

Yes. This hotel has laundry facilities. The hotel also has vending machines, a lobby coffee machine, local newspapers, copy and print services, a fish cleaning station and an observatory. Guests loved the sunrise/sunset views from the private observatory.
