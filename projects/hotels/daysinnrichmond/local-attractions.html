Local Attractions

  • Richmond International Raceway

    Sports enthusiasts will find this irresistible raceway just a 15 minute drive from the Days Inn Richmond. Home to a number of thrilling NASCAR events each year, this raceway provides a perfect venue to get some chills down your spine and get the adrenalin flowing for speed lovers.

  • Pamplin Park and National Museum of Civil War Soldiers

    Three million men fought the Great Civil War and the museum still exhibits their valor, ideologies, lifestyle, their sacrifices and a complete experience of what it was like to be a soldier in those times. Come experience the history in the museum. The museum is minutes away from the hotel.

  • Virginia State Capitol

    The state Capitol houses the oldest legislative body in he United States, the Virginia General Assembly, and is the seat of the state government in the Commonwealth of Virginia. The Virginia State Capitol building is conveniently located within a few miles of the hotel.

  • St. John's Church

    Visit the oldest church in Richmond. The oldest witness of the turmoil during the Civil War. Take a trip into the riches of a serene and spiritual essence of the city where lie the tombstones of some of the most prominent citizens of Richmond.
