Why do hotels charge a pet fee?
Pet fees are charged primarily because hotels have to deep-clean each room after a pet check-out. It may surprise you that housekeeping can spend up to 45 minutes deep-cleaning a pet-friendly room. This is necessary to remove pet hairs, dander, odor and any other residue. Housekeeping bears an additional responsibility of sanitizing bedding items like bedspreads, blankets, comforters, pillows etc. In many cases, carpets may need shampooing or steam-cleaning. A pet fee helps a hotel manage these extra costs.

Does your hotel allow an early check-in?
Occasionally, yes. We only allow early check-ins based on room availability. Our check-in time is 3 P.M. We usually do not allow early check-ins because housekeeping has to make sure that all checked-out rooms for the day are cleaned and inspected. Rooms start getting cleaned after guests check out at 11 A.M. Some guests request late check-outs around 12 P.M. So, most rooms get cleaned and are ready by 2.30 P.M. This is why most hotels have a 3 P.M. check-in time.
