Amenities & Services

Wake up to an aromatic whiff of freshly brewed coffee and a complimentary continental breakfast to kickstart your day after a restful night of sleep. Use our convenient services and amenities to make your stay comfortable and relaxing. So whether you are traveling for business, leisure, or a simple fun family trip, we will make sure that your stay is relaxing and memorable.

Find all the basic amenities within the comfort of your room for a hassle-free stay. Our efficiency rooms feature microwaves, coffee makers, a mini-fridge, hair dryer, iron & ironing board, LCD TV's, a work desk with chair and much more. Reserve our meeeting and conference rooms to make work convenient.

In summer, take a dip and enjoy our seasonal outdoor pool. Our express check-in and check-out saves travelers time. Need help or an extra blanket? Simply dial 0 from your room phone and ask our friendly front desk staff. For your convenience, our front desk works 24 hours.

Why leave your pet at home? Our pet friendly hotel has rooms to accommodate your cat or dog ($20* per day fee applicable). Service animals are free. So, come, bring your pet and enjoy your stay with us.
