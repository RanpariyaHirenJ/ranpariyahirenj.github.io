Getting Here

  • From the North (DC, Fredericksburg)

    1. From I-95 S use the 2nd from the right lane to take exit 79 for I-195 S/I-64 W toward Powhite Pkwy (0.2 mi)
    2. Keep left at the fork, follow signs for Interstate 195 S/Powhite Parkway/U.S. 60/U.S. 360 W (0.4 mi)
    3. Continue onto I-195 S (2.0 mi)
    4. Keep left at the fork to continue on VA-76 S/Powhite Pkwy, follow signs for VA-150/US 60/US 360 (2.2 mi)
    5. Keep left at the fork to continue on VA-76 S (1.3 mi)
    6. Use the left 2 lanes to merge onto VA-150 S/Chippenham Pkwy (1.8 mi)
    7. Take the US-60 E/Midlothian Tnpk exit toward Richmond (0.1 mi)
    8. Follow US-60 E/Midlothian Turnpike, Arcadia St and Atmore Dr to your destination in Richmond (0.7 mi)

  • From The South

    1. Take exit 67B to merge onto VA-150 N/Chippenham Pkwy toward US-60/US-360 W (8.3 mi)
    2. Take the Midlothian Tnpk/US-60 E exit toward Richmond (0.2 mi)
    3. Continue on US-60 E/Midlothian Turnpike to your destination (0.5 mi)
    4. Follow US-60 E/Midlothian Turnpike, Arcadia St and Atmore Dr to the Days Inn Richmond South

  • From Richmond Airport

    1. Follow VA-895 W and VA-150 N to US-60 E/Midlothian Turnpike. Take the Midlothian Tnpk/US-60 E exit from VA-150 N (13.3 mi)
    2. Continue on US-60 E/Midlothian Turnpike to Days Inn Richmond (0.5 mi)
