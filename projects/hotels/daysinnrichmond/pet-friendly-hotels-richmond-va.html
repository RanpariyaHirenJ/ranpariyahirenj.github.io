Pet Friendly Richmond VA Hotel

Days Inn Richmond South at Midlothian offers affordable pet friendly rooms to make you and your pet feel right at home. Dogs and cats are welcome to stay in designated pet-friendly rooms. Our hotel offers free continental breakfast, free high speed WiFi, free local phone calls, in-room microwave/fridge, LCD TV and more. Choose from King Bed & Double Queen Bed spacious rooms. Guests love our exclusive suites and Jacuzzi rooms. The Days Inn Richmond at Midlothian is just 15 minutes away from downtown Richmond VA and close to excellent dining and shopping options.

Hotel Pet Policy

  • Please keep in mind that we have a limited number of designated pet-friendly rooms. Booking in advance is highly recommended to avoid any last minute unavailability.
  • While booking online or over the phone, please make sure to indicate the number of pets that will be accompanying you. If you do not add your pet in your reservation details, we may be unable to honor your request for a pet-friendly room.
  • A $20 daily fee per pet is applicable. A maximum of 2 pets may be allowed in the room. If you have more than 2 pets, please check with the hotel directly before making any reservations. We may, at times, be able to accommodate additional pets, however no guarantees can be made for such requests.
  • Only domestic pets are allowed. This includes dogs and cats. Other pets like rabbits, hamsters, snakes, reptiles, birds or other exotic animals are not allowed.
  • When checking-in, please notify the front desk that you have an accompanyng pet. This is to make sure that you are assigned a pet-friendly room.
  • Pets are not allowed in the breakfast area or the outdoor pool (except in case of service animals)
  • Dogs must be kept on a leash while walking around the property.
  • There is a $200 fine for not disclosing an accompanying pet
  • Pets must be restrained and not pose a danger or act aggressivley towards other guests. Loud barking or aggressive pet behavior can lead to eviction from the hotel property with no pet fee refunds.
  • Pets cannot be left unattended in rooms for long periods.
  • Guests must bring or make arrangements for litter boxes. Our hotel does not provide litter boxes.
  • Property damage fees will be charged if there is any visible damage to the room furniture, linen, walls, carpet, flooring and appliances.
  • Service feees will be charged if excessive cleaning is needed after check-out.
