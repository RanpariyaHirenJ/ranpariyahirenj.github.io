Our Nags Head Hotel

Welcome to the Seahorse Inn & Cottages, (previously the popular Rodeway Inn). Our new management and new name brings with it years of hospitality management experience and dedicated customer satisfaction. Great value for your money is what we strive for. So, when you need a budget hotel for a simple stay, Seahorse Inn & Cottages is truly the perfect choice for travelers. Experience true southern hospitality and a cheerful friendly ambience on every stay. As our valued guest, we aim to make your stay memorable.

  • Hotel Location

    What makes us a travelers preferred hotel is our prime location on the Atlantic ocean. Our Nags Head hotel sits right on the edge of the beach road, very close to US 64 and NC Highway 12.

    Our outer banks hotel is also minutes away from the popular Hatteras Island beaches and the historic Roanoke Island attractions. Within a 2 mile radius are the outlets, watersports and top restaurants that Nags Head has to offer.

  • Rooms & Cottages

    Seahorse Inn & Cottages offers suites, efficiencies and cottages. All our rooms feature LCD TV's with HBO, a modest mini-fridge, microwave and a quick coffee maker. Our outer banks hotel has many room choices to accomodate every vacationer.

    Choose from King bed, Queen, Double beds, Deluxe rooms, rooms with kitchenettes and cottage styled apartments. We also have accessible rooms with convenient easy access for the handicapped. Rooms on the 2nd and 3rd floor have beautiful ocean views from the balcony.

    Relax and unwind on the balcony while enjoying the scenic ocean views sipping coffee or a bottle of wine. If you are here for fishing, make use of our on-site fish cleaning station.

  • Hotel Features

    While you're here, enjoy complimentary freshly brewed coffee, free on premises parking, a picnic area for guests, and our spacious rooms with plush comfortable beds.

    For your added convenience our rooms also feature microwaves, mini-fridges, coffee makers, kitchenettes*, fireplace*, whirlpool tubs*, free Wi-Fi and LCD TVs with HBO.

  • Nearby Attractions

    Our OBX hotel offers you so much to do. Kitty Hawk, made famous by the Wright Brothers' first flight, is a few miles away. The popular Roanoke Island beaches are just 4 miles away.

    In the mood for adventure and exploration? Our Nags Head hotel is minutes away from the First Flight Adventure Park, Nags Head Fishing Pier, Jockeys Ridge State Park, the Roanoke Island Marshes Preserve, Roanoke Island Festival Park, Bodie Island Lighthouse, Wright Brothers National Memorial, North Carolina Aquarium and more!

    You just can't get enough of the Outer Banks. There simply is so much to do. We're sure you and your family will enjoy every moment of your time here.

  • Shopping

    While you're here don't miss the amazing shopping bonanza at the Outlets. Just 0.2 miles away (a 10 min walk) is the famous Tanger Outlet Mall right across the highway.

    Tanger Factory Outlet offers more than 20 top of the line brands at affordable prices. Enjoy a day shopping brands like Gymboree, Ralph Lauren, Claire's, Michael Kors, GAP, Banana Republic, J.Crew, Carter's, Sunglass Hut, Talbots, American Eagle Outfitters, Bass Factory Outlet, Carter's Babies and Kids, Dressbarn, Nautica, Eddie Bauer and more!

  • Restaurants & Dining

    Make the most of your Nags Head vacation by relishing fabulous seafood and southern cuisine at top rated nearby restaurants. If you need a quick bite, Sam & Omie's, a local fisherman's early morning breakfast favorite is just steps away. Cahoon's Market & Deli is across the street and Fatboyz Ice Cream is also a short walk away.

    To truly experience Nags Head's food, head to these highly rated popular restaurants - Owens' Restaurant 0.2 miles, The Dunes Restaurant 0.5 miles, Sugar Creek Seafood Restaurant 0.5 miles, Miller's Waterfront Restaurant 0.6 miles, Basnight's Lone Cedar Cafe 1.3 miles, Fish Heads Bar & Grill 2 miles & Mulligan's Grille 4 miles away.
