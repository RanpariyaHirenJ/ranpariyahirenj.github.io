Our Values

Nags Head, NC
1. Every guest is special.
All our guests receive the same level of personal warm service. We love our guests. It's no wonder that we are rated highly on TripAdvisor and top sites. Our loyal guests have been trusting us for years. We go out of our way to treat our guests as family.
2. Spotless Cleanliness
Immaculate cleanliness and superior customer service. Every room undergoes a deep cleaning with 20 high contact points double sanitizing to keep rooms safe for our guests. Rest assured that your room was deep cleaned and double inspected by the housekeeping manager to make it perfect for your upcoming stay.
3. Low Rates - High Value
Even during peak seasons we strive to keep our rates as low as possible. We believe that our returning loyal guests deserve bigger savings every year. Our pricing is fair. We price match to guarantee the lowest rates. So, you save on every booking with no peak surge pricing.
4. Hospitality Experience
Our hotel management group oversees numerous properties across the Outer Banks. We have 20 years of hospitality industry experience. We know what it takes to nurture every property towards a great customer service experience. Our group understands that our guest truly is our most important asset. We truly care and are always eager to respond to guest requests. We also update and renovate our rooms to keep pace with guest requirements.
