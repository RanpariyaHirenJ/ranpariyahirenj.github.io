Blue Heron Motel - NAGS HEAD - AN OUTER BANKS OCEANFRONT HOTEL

Welcome to the Blue Heron Motel

This summer, make your Nags Head vacation memorable. Our Nags Head hotel offers 30 beautiful ocean facing rooms with unobstructed scenic views of the Atlantic Ocean. And with 2 outdoor oceanfront pools and a heated indoor pool, our Outer Banks hotel offers you a world of affordable pleasure and fun. Here at at our oceanfront Nags Head hotel, you can laze the hours away on our sun-kissed beach, splash and play in the Atlantic's blue waters, or fish for tackle busters from the beach or one of the nearby piers. Your private balcony offers a marvelous sunrise view and unobstructed views of the ocean in all its glory.

Efficiencies offer fully-equipped kitchens and all rooms feature microwaves, mini-refrigerators, courtesy coffee makers and phones.


Planning a vacation with your family? We've got you covered! Feel right at home with our comfortable ocean facing efficiency rooms with fully equipped kitchens to never feel far from home. Our Outer Banks motel also features a heated indoor pool with a spa for those cold chilly nights or when the thunder showers arrive. Simply relax in our indoor pool and melt your stress away.


Enjoy our hot tubs and feel your body rejuvenate in peace and harmony. Make this your home away from home. What's more, our private beach access lets you simply walk to the beach in a few steps. There's tons to do here. Splash in the ocean, go fishing, boating, stand-up paddle boarding, surfing and so much more. Adventure truly beckons you.


Our Outer Banks motel is conveniently located near all major attractions and amenities. Within a 15 min drive is Jennette's Pier (10 min), Jockey's Ridge State Park car (10 min), Roanoke Island Festival Park (13 min), Nags Head Fishing Pier (8 min), Seaside Art Gallery (8 min), Paradise Dolphin Cruises (8 min), Dowdy Park (8 min), Wright Brothers National Memorial (15 min), Island Farm (12 min), OBX Ghost Tours (11 min), Elizabethan Gardens (15 min) and loads of other attractions. Your Outer Banks vacation is guaranteed to be fun-filled.


Nags Head offers a myriad of southern seafood choices. Enjoy fresh catch of the day seafood at popular restaurants and bars nearby. Just a 5 minute walk away are popular local seafood spots like the Dunes Restaurant, Millers Waterfront Restaurant, Owen's Restaurant and Sam and Omie's. Within a 10 min drive, you can find tons of local seafood restaurants that offer delectable menu's that only Nags Head can boast of. And to top it all, enjoy our warm southern hospitality while you're at the hotel.


All our staff have been specially trained to cater to guest needs. Over the years, our hospitality team has built trust and loyalty with our guests. Our housekeeping team makes sure that every room is super-clean and sanitized for your safety. If you need anything, simply pick up the phone and call. Our front desk will do their very best to accommodate your requirements. Experience warm courtesy, smiles and an eagerness to make your stay comfortable and memorable.
