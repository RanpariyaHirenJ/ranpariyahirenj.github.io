FAQ’s

Is the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge pet friendly?

Yes. The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge allows dogs and cats. with a max of 2 pets per room.

How much is the pet fee at Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge charges a pet fee of $25 per pet/day with a maximum of 2 pets per room.

Is Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge the old Duke of Dare Motor Lodge?

Yes. The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge was earlier the Duke of Dare hotel and has been completely renovated and renamed.

How many rooms does the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge have?

Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge has 57 rooms. Rooms include double kings, double queens, single queen and efficiency.

Where is the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge located?

The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge is located at 100 U.S-64 in Manteo NC right next to the El Shaddai Chicken takeout.

What are some of the property amenities at the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

In-room amenities offered at The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge include free Wi-Fi, fridge, microwave, 55" LED TV, DIRECTV channels, coffee maker, work desk w/chair, iron and board, hair dryer, alarm clocks & free parking.

What are the room types at the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offers 3 room types, double king, double queen and single queen rooms.

Can I park my truck or RV at the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

Yes. The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge has ample parking space. Guests can park their RV's and trucks for free.

Is the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge a smoke-free hotel?

Yes. All guest rooms at The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge are non-smoking. Smoking is allowed outdoors in designated areas.

What are the check-in and check-out times at the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

Check-in time is 3 P.M. and the check-out time is 11 A.M. Express check-out is available.

Which restaurants are near the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

El Shaddai Chicken, Pizza Hut, Top China & T.L.'s Family Restaurant are within walking distance to The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge.

What attractions are near the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

Nearby is The Roanoke Marshes Lighthouse, OBX Ghost Tours and the Adventure Museum.

Is the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge in downtown Manteo NC?

Yes. The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge is located in downtown Manteo very close to the waterfront.

What is the cancellation policy at Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge allows free cancellations up to 72 hours before the check-in date and time.

Does the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offer monthly stays?

Yes. The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offers affordable weekly, monthly and long stay options. Please call to confirm availability.

Does the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge have accessible rooms?

The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge currently does not offer any accessible rooms.

Does the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge have handicap parking?

Yes. The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge has limited handicapped parking spots.

What payment methods does the does the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offer?

The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge accepts cash, Visa, MasterCard, Amex & Discover credit cards and all major debit cards.

Does the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offer AARP, military and veteran discounts?

Yes, the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offers AARP, military and veteran discounts.

How old do I have to be to book a room at The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

You must be over the age of 21 to book a room at The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge.

Does The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge allow group reservations?

Yes, The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offers group reservations based on availability.

Does The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offer corporate discounts?

Yes, The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offers corporate discounts to employees of businesses who have partnered with the hotel. Please check with your employer.

Does The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offer long stays?

Yes, The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offers affordable long stay options especially from October through April.

Does The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offer cribs?

Yes, The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offers cribs based on availability. Please call in advance to request a crib.

Does The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offer rollaway beds?

Yes, The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offers rollaway beds for extra guests. There is a $50/day fee for rollaway beds. Rollaway beds are subject to availability.

Does The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge have a swimming pool?

No. The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge does not feature a swimming pool.

Does The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge offer rooms with kitchenettes?

Yes. The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge has limited efficiency rooms with a kitchenette.

Does The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge have laundry facilities?

No. The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge currently does not have any on-site laundry facility. But, the Wash House laundromat is a 2 minute walk away.

Is there a grocery store near The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

Yes, the Piggly Wiggly is right next door to the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge.

Is there a laundromat near The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge?

Yes, the Wash House laundromat is right next to The Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge.
