The History, the Memories, now Reborn in the Heart of Manteo.

A vintage hotel with history comes back to life in Manteo, NC. The Duke of Dare Motor Lodge is now the Heart of Manteo Motor Lodge. We did our best to retain the old world charm and surroundings while adding a modest refinement to the living spaces. We are conveniently located just minutes away from the waterfront boardwalk and top attractions in downtown Manteo.

Our hotel in Manteo NC with 56 newly remodeled rooms offers all the standard room amenities to make your stay comfortable. Located in the heart of downtown Manteo, we are just minutes away from popular attractions like the Roanoke Marshes Lighthouse, OBX Ghost Tours and the waterfront boardwalk. Nearby are top local restaurants to experience succulent fresh seafood that will keep you craving for more. Need groceries? Shop right next door at the Piggly Wiggly.

We welcome you to come experience our modest Outer Banks hotel and enjoy the best Manteo has to offer. From our heart to your heart ... The Heart of Manteo!
