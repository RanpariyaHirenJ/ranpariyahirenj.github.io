About

The Inn is nestled in the village of Buxton on Hatteras Island, often referred to as "The Outer Banks". THE HATTERAS ISLAND INN sits on the beach side of Highway 12 and it is only steps to the beach. An observation tower at the hotel allows guests an awesome view that goes on forever.
