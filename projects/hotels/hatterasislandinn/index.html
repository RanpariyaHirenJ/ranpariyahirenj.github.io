Welcome to the Hatteras Island Inn

Outer Banks Hotel

5 Min Walk to the Beach - 4 Min Drive to Cape Hatteras Lighthouse

The Inn is nestled in the village of Buxton on Hatteras Island, often referred to as "The Outer Banks". Our Outer Banks hotel sits on the beach side of Highway 12 and is only steps away from the beach. Guests love the observation tower at the hotel which allows guests a panoramic views & offers magnificent sunset experiences.

Enjoy 60 well appointed, comfortable rooms featuring high speed Wi-Fi, Microwaves, Refrigerators, Coffee Makers, Iron w/ironing boards & large LCD TV's with popular channels. Our hotel observation tower is a guest favorite. Enjoy a splash in our refreshing outdoor pool & enjoy your family vacation. Other convenient amenities include an on-site laundry & vending machines.
