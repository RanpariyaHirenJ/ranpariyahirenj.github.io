COVID-19 Measures

COVID-19 Precautionary Measures

In an effort to contain the spread of COVID-19, Colonial Inn Motel is in the process of implementing guest safety & precautionary measures. Some of these safety practices are already in place. We are making our best efforts to comply with federal, local and state safety best practices to ensure guest and employee safety

  • Plexiglass shield at front desk
  • No in-room services (for guests who are in house right now) – guests can come to the front desk and get items they may need for their continued stay
  • No daily housekeeping service. Guests can request linen and towel changes by coming to the front desk.
  • Once a guest checks out, the room is left empty for 4-5 days before it is cleaned. Before cleaning, the room temperature is set at high heat to kill any residual bacteria.
  • All staff is wearing gloves and masks and are continuously washing their hands throughout their shifts
  • Housekeeping staff changes gloves after each task (1 set of gloves for stripping the bed, 1 set of gloves for cleaning the bathroom, 1 set of gloves for making the beds, etc.)
  • Using Lysol wipes and spray along with suggested disinfectant/cleaning products
  • Hand sanitizer stations on all floors of the property
  • Dirty laundry is completed by the end of day – not letting it stay in the carts
  • Each time a guest comes to the front desk and leaves, the desk and touch point areas are immediately wiped down (including elevator buttons, luggage racks, hand rails, vending machines, any other touch points)
  • Key cards are being cleaned daily
  • Floors are being mopped continuously
  • All high touch and public spaces are being sanitized and disinfected every 2 hours (they plan to continue this process as long as needed)
  • Social distancing measures as directed by the state
  • Dedicated lobby attendant wiping down all public areas
  • Cleaning all surfaces with a virucide and then disinfecting with a commercial Lysol type product
  • Opening doors and windows as much as possible to ventilate and to keep air circulating
  • Using cleaning products specifically recommended by the EPA for COVID19
  • Housekeeping is being retrained in a more vigorous cleaning of all surfaces in a room ( for example touch points in rooms – making sure to sanitize light switches, remote controls, safes, etc.)
  • Staff is changing out of their uniforms prior to leaving from their shift

The Colonial Inn is open year round and shows its old world charm as it proudly stands as one of the oldest motels on the beach in Nags Head, North Carolina.
