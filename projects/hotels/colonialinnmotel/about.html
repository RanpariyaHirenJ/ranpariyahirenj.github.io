About Us

Overlooking the clear waters of the Atlantic Ocean on Nags Head, the Colonial Inn offers travelers modest and affordable accomodation. We have rooms for every budget and feature select apartment style rooms with full kitchens. Our 2 bedroom apartment rooms are popular with families who love the extra space and room to feel right at home.

Experience southern charm and hospitality at our hotel.

All our staff have years of hospitality experience and are trained to offer guests the very best in service. Being one of the oldest motels in Nags Head, we have built trust and loyal customers over time. Our loyal customers leave delighted each time and keep coming back every year to relive fun-filled vacations and build lasting memories of good times.

Let us delight you with our warm smiles and attention to detail. Come, let loose at the Colonial Inn today!
