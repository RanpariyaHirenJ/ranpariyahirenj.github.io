Welcome to the Colonial Inn

Nags Head Beachfront Hotel

The Colonial Inn features perfect rooms for every traveler. From standard rooms to efficiencies & 2 bedroom apartments with a full kitchen, our rooms make the perfect choice for singles, couples and large families. Our Outer Banks hotel is pet friendly, so you don't need to leave your loved pets at home. Come enjoy our beautiful modest rooms with pool & ocean views. All rooms feature standard amenities including free high speed Wi-Fi, microwaves, fridges, LCD TV's, alarm clocks, iron & board, coffee maker & more. Guests love our sun deck which offers beautiful clear views of the beach. Enjoy our seasonal outdoor pool to relax and cool off. In the mood to barbecue? Our outdoor barbecues are free to use and enjoy great times.

The best of Nags Head food and dining is just within walking distance. Waveriders Coffee Deli Pub, Austin Fish Company, Tortugas' Lie Shellfish Bar & the Lucky 12 Tavern are within a 10 minute walking distance. Nags Head fishing pier is a 1 min walk. The historic Jockey's Ridge State Park is just a 13 minute walk. While you're here, enjoy the Outer Banks at it's finest. Guests love our attention to detail and courteous southern charm. Come happy, leave delighted! Come, experience the Colonial Inn charm today..
