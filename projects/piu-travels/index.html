Forex

One of the main difficulties pertaining to a trip abroad involves the rigorous currency exchange routine one has to undergo before the trip and post the trip. To facilitate easy conversion of  money, Piu Travels undertakes this job for you to ensure you are never short of cash on a trip. The actual process requires heavy investment in terms of time and man power and our busy schedules hardly allow us space for such errands. As a travelling agency catering to all your needs, we provide fast and immediate conversion of currency so that you can perform any transaction at any time according to your convenience. We also, simultaneously, look after your finances to prevent you from exceeding your budget.

MICE

In the contemporary world today, travelling is not just synonymous with holidays or vacations. In fact, most of the travelling today are related to important work or business related trips. The main concept of MICE is bringing a moderate to a large number of people together for a specific purpose. Thus, trips and tourisms of such genres are planned well in advance, depending thoroughly on the scale of the tour and it takes rigorous detailing. Piu Travels looks to resolve all your hassles regarding such grand scale tours and trips to ensure a smooth running of trips that do not interfere with or jeopardise your work in any possible way.
