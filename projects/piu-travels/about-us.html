About Us

Life is similar to a journey in ways more than one. All we need is have enough heart to discover and explore. Piu Travels, strongly believes in this ideal. Hence, we have taken it on ourselves to make every journey you take an experience to treasure and remember.  Piu Travel came into existence in 2005 as a group of young and spirited travel enthusiasts came together to improve the tourism culture and there was no looking back ever since. Piu Travels have achieved recognition, love from our long term associates and government affiliation. Thus, we are closer to making travelling as convenient as possible for people who love it.

Our Story

Piu Travels, one of India's leading and well known travelling agencies, came into being back in 2005, when Mr. Bapan Mondal, a genuine travel enthusiast,  decided to make any sort of travelling experience as comfortable as possible for people. Over the decade, Piu Travels has showed expertise and merit in every sphere related to the tourism industry, both national and international. From hotels and car rentals, from leisure travelling to grand scale MICE tourism, from facilitating foreign exchange to passport or visa assistance , Piu Travel has been appreciated for coming to the aid of their customers in their hours of need.

  • Our Founder

    Mr. Bapan Mondal

    A student of political science in Calcutta University, ventured into a business of fertilisers, which, till date , exists. His debut into the travelling business was driven by the simple idea that people should have easy access and reliable resources to booking tickets for travelling.

  • CEO

    Mr. Aseem Biswas

    Piu Travels made his mark in the tourism business at the very start of his career. He was always focused on bringing about a concrete change in the evolution of the business and facilitate easy travelling for all purposes for his clients. Years of experience in tourism business, hotels, sales and marketing has contributed immensely to his compassionate and efficient approach towards the subject.
