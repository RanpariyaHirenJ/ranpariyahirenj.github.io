About

Episode One

Fusing a legacy that has been around for three decades with innovation & all things new, Mr. Anjan Chatterjee & Avik Chatterjee, bring to Kolkata a one of a kind resto-bar serving delectable food from all over the world along with some beautifully crafted taptails. Welcome to Episode. Season One. Episode One.

“Episode One is defined by everything it isn’t”

Not snobbish, not stiff, not inaccessible.
We talk the language of Gymkhanas and Clubs and we turn on its head.
Walk into Episode One, our first of many to follow and you will be greeted by a beautiful mélange of old school meets contemporary.
Episode One is today’s take on a Gymkhana or the British Club.
Episode One desires to build a creative community bringing together like minded individuals from all walks of life including art, music, fashion, films, media, technology and more.

The very first thing that catches the eye will be the state of the art bar which will be brewing delicious taptails (cocktails on tap) all day long! Episode is divided into 4 experiences – each one serving a different purpose and can be enjoyed by everyone alike.

  • The Tap Room : with state of the art & never done before “Taptails”
  • The Library: work meetings, book readings & private Dining by night.
  • The Chambers: rejuvenate with a head massage, hair styling or a face mask.
  • The Veranda : converse over high tea or sheesha (smoking zone)
  • The Ballroom: Dance like nobody’s watching on our specially curated event calendar with the best upcoming artists internationally & in India.

    • Episode stands for yesterday’s positives (nostalgia) paired with today’s lifestyle & trends and how the contrast eventually works together to bring out the same simplistic feelings in humanity.

    TAPTAILS
    It is a one of its kind, first in its class - TAP ROOM in the world - where bespoke cocktail taanks were built for Episode One to disrupt the current trend of cocktail making in India. Taptails is the future of Indian bar and we are the innovation leaders.

    WORLD CUISINE
    You don't need to travel all across the country; Episode One has satisfactory palettes ranging from Indian, Turkish, Continental, Thai & Asian food. Amongst the Desi Poutine, Avocado Tacos, the Lasa Fish Tikka, N.R.I. Butter Chicken and the sushi, you'll find the zestiness of salads and the richness of an Indian curry; the heat of Thai food and the use-your-hands snackiness of tapas. Everything tastes like a fiesta in your mouth. Episode One also introduces High Tea/Tipsy Tea which has the most intriguing and delectable selection of exquisite ‘Champagne’ and ‘Spirited’ teas.

    Welcome to Episode One, now in Kolkata.
