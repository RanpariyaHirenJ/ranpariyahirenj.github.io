About

Gong Modern Asian

If innovative creative menu which amalgamates global flavours is what you look for in your plate, GONG is the perfect place to sample some of the best of fusion food. So when Italian or South American food is married to Asian flavours you get some out of the world dishes like Asian Crab Ravioli, Asparagus Crisp Rolls or the Chilean Sea Bass in Tamari Soy. Yes it will intrigue you with its tantalising tastes and completely nouveau recipes!

The interiors are pure Japanese style architecture and design with dynamic lighting and intense quiet dining spaces that have soft luxuriant seating arrangements.

Gong currently has one outlet in Pune, but dreams to go national.

Let the fusion of fantastic flavours reverberate in your senses in GONG!
