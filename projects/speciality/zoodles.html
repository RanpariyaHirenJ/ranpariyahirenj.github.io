About

Zoodles

A quick on-the-go, fun, Oriental street food experience is what Zoodles offers to its diners.

The tantalizing aromas, the tangy taste of the sauces, the fast funky moves of the Chefs in the kitchen who whip up fresh food stirred and sautéed into perfection that will remind you of the terrific food options of the streets of Kuala Lumpur, or Bangkok!

Some of the specialities include Zoodle Momos like Sriracha Mayo, Schezwan Momos, Cheese Corn Pepper Blast and Teriyaki Chicken Wingers among starters, Holy Basil & Schezwan Fury flavours among the specialized Zoodle Boxes and the classic Hakka Street Noodles and Butter Garlic Noodles with the fresh zing and twist of street flavours.

Zoodles is your perfect place to hit while being on the move in the city!

Eat Funky! Go Zoodles !
