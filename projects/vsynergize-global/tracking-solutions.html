Tracking Solutions

Today for businesses to operate efficiently, it is important to have greater visibility and traceability of devices, people and things.

Location data enables businesses to track assets across the world. It is possible to achieve better performances and improve customer satisfaction by reducing downtime, reducing risk, scheduling preventative maintenance, and obtaining real-time intelligence of a remote asset's operation.

Real-time access to data from remote sensors and tracking devices makes it possible to take corrective action before a loss or unplanned downtime can occur.

At VSynergize Global, we develop solutions ranging from providing raw location information to offering fully connected ecosystems for location data aggregation. We develop comprehensive and customized IoT-enabled location tracking solutions for multiple industries.

Industry Use Cases where
tracking solution
can be deployed

01

Fleet - Management

A tracking solution improves capabilities and enables real-time vehicle information for efficient fleet scheduling and utilization, route tracking, fuel use, and vehicle maintenance. Visibility about vehicle location and status also reduces operating costs and improves customer service.

Healthcare - People Tracking & machineries, equipment, ambulances, instrument

Wearable-based people tracking solutions allow caregivers to track the location of patients and receive emergency alerts or notifications in cause of an emergency.

02

Logistics - Container Tracking & goods

Tracking solutions help logistics and supply chain professionals manage container shipments across various modes of transportation efficiently. Automated alerts go off if containers do not arrive as scheduled, and also prevents theft and ensures shipment security.

Industrial - Inventory and Resource Tracking

Tracking solutions in industrial environments enable access to real-time location of raw input and final output inventory items, forklifts, pallets, and other equipments. Also wearable solutions for workers will ensure safety in warehouses or on the field.

03

Construction - Material and Equipment Tracking

With tracking solutions for building materials, tools, and heavy equipment, construction companies can immediately determine material availability and location across huge or dispersed sites.

Automotive - Stolen Vehicle Recovery

Tracking solutions can protect the security of drivers and their vehicles with stolen vehicle recovery services.
