How it Works

  • You can choose to invest in Quint and grow with us. We guarantee our Seed Investors a 3x / 3.5x of their investment within the first 6 months of the Launch depending on whether you opt for Partial or Panoptic Vesting.

    (e.g. USDT 1m now with Partial Vesting  USDT 3m in 6 months with progressive pay-outs)
    (e.g. USDT 1m now with Panoptic Vesting  USDT 3.5m in 6 months with a one-off pay-out)

  • The Vesting of the token will be for 6 months with the first month being the Breather.
    • If the investor opts for Partial Vesting, he will be able to claim 20% of the total tokens allocated at the end of the second month and respectively 20% every month-end that follows till the end of the 6th month
    • If the investor opts for Partial Vesting, he will be able to claim 100% of the total tokens allocated as a single pay-out at the end of the 6th month
  • The Claiming of the released tokens can be as USDT or QUINT token (equivalent of the corresponding USDT value on the day). This will be released to your wallet.
  • The Seed Investors can claim their tokens here: quint.io/seed. They can either opt to continue to Stake their claimed tokens in one of our Staking Pools or withdraw them to their wallet as USDT or QUINT (equivalent of the corresponding USDT value on the day).
The Vesting period is categorised into, PARTIAL: PANOPTIC: EXECUTION:
  • Connect your Wallet
  • Navigate to Contribute Seed
  • Make sure you have USDT in the wallet
  • Fill in the form
  • Choose your vesting method
  • Choose you return method
  • Enter the “Your Contribution” value
  • Voila!
