The Ritz Life. 95 Canal Heights comes with its very own privileged features. Starting with the fact that there are only 9 residences, one on each floor. Every detail of the building is masterfully curated. From the paintings in the lobby and first floor to the wall in the banquet and the lighthouse beacon in the garden.

Grand Entrance. Guests are greeted at the grand lobby. A place that not only exudes elegance, but also has a lounge to entertain, while they wait to be ushered upstairs. What’s more, even their cars have private parking spaces.

Banquet Hall. Celebrate all your life events in the exclusive banquet hall that can accommodate up to a 100 guests. Add glamour, ambience, and style to your lavish soirees.

Energy Zone. A fully-equipped 'LifeFitness (USA)' gym and a well-appointed luxurious sauna, all of these to pamper a handful of residents.

Electronic Surveillance As a private haven for the privileged, the property boasts of all that is required to make its residents feel secure and relaxed - CCTV vigillance, fire safety systeem and stringent security protocols.

The creative minds behind the project,
Roshni and Rahul Jhaveri.

Roshni and Rahul Jhaveri own the jewelry design atelier Studio Renn in Mumbai. Their remarkably distinctive jewelry is highly valued among the cognoscenti across the world.
With 95 canal Heights, Nagpur, the Jhaveris have channelised their creative energy into creating an aesthetic residence, resplendent with energy and beauty. The twist-form profile of the building and large internal spaces play with symmetry and asymmetry to create a bijou home that transforms living to an everyday wonder and joyous experience. Impeccable workmanship, obsessive attention to detailing, the greenery wrap, top-of-line fixtures and finishes make this a true collector’s home.
