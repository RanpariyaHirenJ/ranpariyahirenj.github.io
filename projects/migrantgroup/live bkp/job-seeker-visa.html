Job Seeker Visa

For all those looking to expand their business Worldwide and looking for staff for their company, a job seeker visa is the best option. All those citizens of the EU, they can easily work in Portugal without any limitations or without applying for a visa.

Understand all job seeker visa requirements and other things from the one and only team at Migrant Group. Here you will meet a team with extensive experience handling queries related to the job seeker visa.

Things to have while applying for the job seeker visa in Portugal

  • When you are applying for a job seeker visa in Portugal, you must have a job offer from a particular company.
  • Your employer must apply for the work permit on your presence

Secure
