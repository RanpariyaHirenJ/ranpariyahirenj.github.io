About Us

Innovatiove and Creative

We Are FeliFarma

FeliFarma is a specialty pharmaceutical company engaged in the development, manufacturing, and marketing of world class pharmaceutical and surgical products. We are committed to 'Serve Health Care Needs Worldwide'. Our product range is customized to each market we are present in. We clearly understand our customer’s needs and use innovative technology to present state of the art solutions. Our business incorporates Branded Generics in the developing markets of Asia and Africa, as well as Generics in Europe and North America.

We have designed a customized basket of products for each of our markets and serve a wide range of therapeutic segments like Anti-Biotic, Anti-Malarial, Anti-Diabetic, Cardiology, Gynecology, Orthopaedics, Pediatrics, Respiratory & General Health products etc.

Vision

Our Vision is to serve all mankind with effective and quality medicines. To maintain world class manufacturing and distribution facilities and to ensure timely and efficient delivery of medical and surgical products by our team of professionals and highly trained experts.

At every step we shall fulfil the guidelines of cGMP (Current Good Manufacturing Practice) and all regulatory requirements.
