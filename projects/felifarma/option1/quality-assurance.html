Quality Assurance

We cover all aspects that could have an impact on the quality of prescribed pharmaceutical products

Quality Assurance (QA) is a wide concept and covers all aspects that could have an impact on the quality of prescribed pharmaceutical products. Our objectives of QA are: to ensure that the prescribed medicine competently provides the desired effect to the person taking it and to ensure medicines comply with the international regulations.

Feli Farma strives to provide high quality products to enable them to enhance our reputation, maximise profit and to provide high quality drugs to humans. To meet these targets, they rely on well-designed PQS, which involve the coordination of quality through processes, with the aim of producing finished products of the highest quality.

Quality Assurance

  • The pharmaceutical product is designed to meet the need and performance requirements
  • The process is designed to consistently meet product critical quality attributes
  • Processes, equipment, personnel and deviations are identified and controlled in an appropriate manner
  • The whole manufacturing process is constantly monitored and updated to enable consistency in quality over time.
  • the specific methodology which will be used to test a new product
  • a complete assessment of laboratory's conformance with GMP's

In accordance with FDA quality requirements, Felifarma has established a strict and sound quality management system to insure the all standardised operations of testing and inspections, and to ensure the integrity and tractability of data.


Each product goes through a strict product quality check and analysis, highest quality standards are the key driver of Felifarma success.

Accreditation

All Felifarma products manufactured with WHO and GMP certified manufacturer. All are manufacturing plants are WHO-GMP approved, equipped with state-of-the-art technology to manufacture products as per International standards. Quality check-ins are in place to ensure that every step of manufacturing process and equipments are complaint with the strict WHO GMP standards.

Packaging

Packaging is a critical tool in the pharmaceutical industry and we have been taking its utmost care at Felifarma. We ensure that our products packing is of high standard and sake for the product which will keep the product from any contamination and will reach to the patents in impeccable condition.
