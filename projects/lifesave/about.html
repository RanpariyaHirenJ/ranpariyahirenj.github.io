LifeSafe hygiene products come from 108 Arogyam Group. The group has over two decades of immersive experience in pharmaceutical products, business, sales and distribution. Quality assurance, hygiene, high-grade manufacturing and end-to-end compliance are intrinsic to 108’s operations. In course of that journey, the LifeSafe brand offers a suite of consumer products that delivers complete peace of mind to its users. The products are developed through meticulous research, technical testing and consumer testing and complies with the demanding requirements of Indian, European and US Federal authorities in the category.

108 Arogyam also manufactures white label products for quality conscious businesses in India and abroad. Meeting exacting demands of clients and the various regulatory bodies they deal with is a further testimony to 108’s high quality approach.

manufacturing

LifeSafe products are manufactured in state of the art facilities, in a positive pressure sealed factory. End to end Touch free manufacturing ensures the strictest hygiene level. The automated machines are capable of high production output.
