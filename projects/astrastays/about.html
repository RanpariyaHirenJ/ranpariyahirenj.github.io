Ajit Koushik

x

Ajit Koushik founded Astra  with a clear vision to be the corporate world's partner of choice for their executive housing & hospitality services needs.  In this pursuit, he is supported by the family office of Mr. Cyrus P. Mistry.

 A Hotel Management graduate from the Institute Hotelier Cesar Ritz, Switzerland, Ajit brings over 25 years of experience in hospitality and managed services environments, having worked with global brands in Europe, GCC & India.

Ajit was until November 2017 the India head of Singapore based The Ascott Ltd., the world's largest international serviced residence owner-operator.

Leveraging his multifaceted operational experience, Ajit helms Astra operations with a singular focus of delivering an unrivalled level of quality and customized service,  a clear expectation of today's business executive.
