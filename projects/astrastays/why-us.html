What Sets Us Apart

Newer construction

Our apartments are in recently constructed buildings with a modern set-up which contributes to an exceptional quality of living.

Self contained

Each individual apartment is aesthetically designed, featuring a separate living room, bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, a well equipped kitchen and generous storage space.

Emergency Power

All our apartments are equipped with emergency electricity backup to ensure uninterrupted power.

Security

We take the security of our guests very seriously and adhere to strict monitoring of the building's access controls to ensure their safety.

Fire safety

We have a hi-tech addressable Fire Alarm System in the building and individual apartments comprising of heat and smoke detectors. This helps to prevent any accidental fires by detecting smoke and heat early.

Wellness features

With the focus on good health and fitness on everyone's mind, our buildings feature a swimming pool and a fully equipped inhouse gym with instructors. A personal trainer can also be arranged for, if required.
