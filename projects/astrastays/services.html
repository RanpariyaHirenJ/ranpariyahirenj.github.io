Our Services

Furniture, Fixtures and Equipment

We, at Astra, understand the importance of feeling at home in the midst of luxury. We provide furniture, fixtures and equipment customized to the likes and preferences of the residents who will make it their home for the duration of their stay.

Hospitality Services

We maintain the strictest standards and offer internationally benchmarked hospitality services.

  • Our housekeeping team takes care of the daily cleaning of the apartment.
  • For any maintenance issues within the apartment, our engineering team remains on call maintenance.
  • Our concierge service takes care of all business or pleasure needs of Astra residents, be it running a simple errand or to fulfilling a complex, time-consuming request.
  • We offer regular bulletins on cross cultural awareness to easily adapt to the local culture.

Good Sleep Experience

The advantages of a good night's sleep are universally known. Our 12 inch rebonded foam mattresses and feather pillows ensure that nothing comes in the way of your comfortable and sound sleep at night.

Travel Desk

Sometimes getting about in a new city can be troublesome. Our travel desk is totally dedicated to taking care of office transfers as well as other personal conveyance needs of residents and their families, whenever needed.

Food & Beverage

Nothing completes a homestay like home food. At Astra, upon your request, we have the facility to provide you with country specific food and cuisine, along with your favourite drink.

Entertainment

We ensure the best in-house entertainment is available for our residents, including but not limited to Cable TV with international news and entertainment channels as well as on-demand streaming options.

Guest Service Management System

Our Guest Services team has refined workflows for all residents service requests through a Guest Service Management system to ensure consistent, efficient and personalized support.
