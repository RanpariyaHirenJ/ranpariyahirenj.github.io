The HUB

Vehicle Tracking Solution

You need to know where your vehicles are, when, and to make sure they (and your drivers) are protected at all times. The HUB is an ADAPT Ideations design which does just that—protect, simply. A multifunctional IoT device that comes integrated with a suite of tracking and security sensors, the HUB can be retro-fit to any vehicle and comes in the following modules:

  1. The ADAPT Lite Hub (3 sensor)
  2. The ADAPT Eco Hub (5 sensor)
  3. Ultra Hub Multi sensor (7+ sensor)

Vehicle Tracking Solution

The HUB can interface with on board diagnostics—when your car is due for servicing, information about the odometer, about the behaviour of the driver— whether they braking too often—and transmit all this information back to your central location in real time. The vehicle therefore becomes a true Smart Car.

You can take just what solutions you need, or have all the bells and whistles; the HUB is therefore scalable to your business needs—whether you've got a fleet, or a single vehicle. The HUB provides a complete vehicle tracking and protection solution.

OPTIMIZED PERFORMANCE

The device is customisable with sensors to fit your needs:

  1. Temperature—ideal for cold chain monitoring, and tracking of changing temperature conditions.
  2. GPS—tracking and alerts for moving, high value assets with geo-fencing (predefined set of geographic boundaries).
  3. Geo-Fence Alerts—works with the GPS sensor to proactively monitor your vehicle and asset behaviour. You'll receive alerts if your vehicles leave a predefined geographic location.
  4. Vehicle Immobilizer—the ability to stall the engine of a vehicle remotely.
  5. Panic Button—touch button driver protection sends an emergency alert to your head office or other chosen location.
  6. Electro-magnetic Lock—remote management of access and security to entry and exit of business vehicles.
