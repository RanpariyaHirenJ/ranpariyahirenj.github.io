Lone Worker Alarm

Worker safety is paramount

Making sure your workers return home safely to their families at the end of the day is a business's most basic responsibility. Lone workers have many faces in many different industries, and may include not only those who work alone, but also those who work without direct supervision in an isolated environment.

Smart Tagger

ADAPT Ideations Lone Worker Alarm combines the latest LoRa and fall alarm technologies, to provide your employees with confidence and protection at the touch of a button.

OPTIMIZED PERFORMANCE

High risk lone worker activities/industries include:

  1. Working at heights
  2. Forestry
  3. Truck or delivery drivers
  4. Carers
  5. Real estate inspectors
  6. Confined spaces
  7. Working with hazardous substances or materials
  8. Underground Rail
  9. Security
  10. Telecommunications

Our aim is to utilize innovative technology solutions to improve the safety of lone workers no matter where they are. As a business entity you need to consider: are your lone workers safe?
