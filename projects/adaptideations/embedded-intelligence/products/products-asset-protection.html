Asset Protection

Smart Tagging Equals Smart Business

Things go missing. They end up somewhere they shouldn't be. They get stolen. It's a way of life. But constant business losses don't have to be. ADAPT Ideations provide easy, ready-made, affordable solutions to keep track of your returnable assets such as pallets, portable toilets, recycling bins and telecommunications cable reels.

Smart Tagger

Simply attach an ADAPT Ideations Smart Tagger and you'll enable the visibility of any asset, anywhere, at any time. You'll automatically receive GPS position data, and notifications when your asset is moved beyond your designated geo-fencing area. This provides the opportunity for proactive intervention, and ultimately results in a much more accurate asset pool, while also reducing unnecessary business expense.

OPTIMIZED PERFORMANCE

Use ADAPT Ideations Smart Tagger for tracking:

  1. Fibre optics
  2. High value copper cables
  3. Pallets
  4. Waste recycling bins
  5. Portable toilets
  6. Retail trolleys

Using a Smart Tagger isn't just smart. It's also good business. It's not just about utilizing technology for the sake of it, but offering a technology solution that delivers powerful business information, while providing previously unattainable visibility and control over your asset pool.
