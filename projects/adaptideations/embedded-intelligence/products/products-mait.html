Mechatronic Ai Track (MAIT)

Your Own Mars Rover!

The rover may have been popularised on Mars, but surveying faraway landscapes in the search for alien life isn't its sole application. This Smart RoV (Remote Operated Vehicle) from ADAPT Ideations is a unique combination of robotic engineering and IoT, and is designed to automate repetitive, labour intensive tasks in the home, retail and commercial space.

DATA DRIVEN ROVER

MAiT isn't a fully functional robot, rather, it is an advanced remotely operated IoT device that can perform specific programmable actions under given conditions. Mechatronics offers integration via the internet—enabling you to transmit a great deal of valuable information easily and effectively from the rover, to your office (or wherever you might be). This makes the product a data-driven, value-orientated proposition.

OPTIMIZED PERFORMANCE

Case uses for MAiT include:

  1. Aged care monitoring and support—such as helping elderly people in their daily chores.
  2. Retail concierge—transformation of a consumer's shopping experience through assisted shopping.
  3. Security guard—physical protection of facilities or assets through a constant electronic presence.
  4. Industrial store person—performing repetitive warehouse chores, such as stock take or reverse logistics.
