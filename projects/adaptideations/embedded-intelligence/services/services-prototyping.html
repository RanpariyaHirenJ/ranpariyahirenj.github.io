Prototyping

A Real Product in Your Hands

Prototyping is an integral part of our R.A.P.I.D. methodology and the first concrete step in translating an idea into reality. A prototype shouldn't be looked upon as an expense—rather an investment where you get to gain valuable information, provide feedback to the developer and design team and learn lessons swiftly. Often a prototype is the quickest way to understand the feasibility of a solution without burning valuable capital.

R.A.P.I.D PROTOTYPES

ADAPT Ideations specialize in developing fast, workable prototypes with complete flexibility in technology and design. The prototyping stage is an exciting phase of the innovation journey where the DNA of a brilliant idea is put to the test to assess operational function and market endurance.

KEY BENEFITS

The key benefits of Prototyping are:

  1. More practical design features and an end device that will be far closer to what the user wants. Devices need to be used in real world situations.
  2. Expedition of the speed-to-market cycle. Timeliness is critical.
  3. A cost effective and smart way to test the waters-as opposed to a leap of faith.

While your device is on paper (or PC), it's still just a dream. Once you have a physical prototype investors, and other decision makers take you (and your product) much more seriously.
