Consumer Mechatronics

Beyond the Buzzword

One of the buzziest buzzwords today is Mechatronics. So let's keep it plain and simple. Mechatronics is (as the name suggests) based upon a blend of mechanical engineering and electronics (with a smattering of software development thrown in for good measure), yet it's also a distinct field from all three.

INTELLIGENT MECHANICAL MACHINES

Mechatronics deals with the addition of intelligence to mechanical machines, and/or replacing mechanical machines with an intelligent electronic solution to free up humans and improve business efficiency. Essentially, Mechatronics is all about delivering the best possible product or service experience to the end user—your customer. Past applications of mechatronics which have benefited the consumer include the autofocus camera, vehicle cruise control, advanced manufacturing automation, and—the Mars Rover!

OPTIMIZED PERFORMANCE

Key benefits of Mechatronics to your business include:

  1. Optimized performance, productivity, and reliability for your products, services and systems.
  2. Enhanced, user-friendly features and functionality.
  3. Increased flexibility for changes in requirements.
  4. Cost-effectiveness.

Mechatronics has the potential to touch every industry-smart consumer products, medicine, defence, manufacturing, telecommunication-and to create vast opportunities for your business to save costs, increase your bottom line, and give you a competitive edge.
