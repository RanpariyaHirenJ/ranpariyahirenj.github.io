SUCCESS STORIES

  • Packaging as a Service

    ADAPT's Hub Lite redefines operational excellence in the packaging industry by providing accurate, real-time data for consumable products, and preventative alerts for machine maintenance.

  • The Problem

    A leading Australian supply chain services company was faced with the challenge of improving the accuracy of their billing of packaging material to clients. Previously, the process had been carried out manually—which was continually resulting in human errors, as well as issues around timeliness of information. While small—item-wise, this was adding up long term to a negative impact on both the company's profitability, and customer service delivery.

  • Enter, ADAPT

    First they needed to accurately gauge packaging material consumption. ADAPT's HUB Eco was fitted to packaging machines and trialled for 90 days at one of the supply chain company's most challenging client sites.

    The analogue counter readings from the packaging machine were converted and transmitted via ADAPT's Hub Lite Smart Sensors on a real time basis to the ADAPT server, and the results presented using ADAPTer (ADAPT'S proprietary data analysis software).

    The result—the analytics not only provided up-to-the-minute, accurate, readings of the packaging machine counters, but also revealed the productivity statistics of the machines. When one machine went down the supply chain company's support team was alerted before the client even reported the problem. A game-changer to the company—who instantly saw the potential savings in machine down-time, combined with real time data at their fingertips.

  • Today

    ADAPT Hub Lite has been rolled out at 102 client sites across 340 machines in Australia, delivering the on-hand data needed for accurate billing. ADAPTer is now being redeveloped to offer deeper analytical data to assist the supply chain company's support team plan efficient maintenance and other proactive steps.
