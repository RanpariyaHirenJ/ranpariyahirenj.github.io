Remote Operated Vehicle

  • Remote Operated Vehicle

    MAiT (Mechatronic Ai Track) is a Remote Operated Vehicle (ROV) purpose-built to carry out a range of everyday tasks, and is the result of 24 months of research and development by our Mechatronics partners Janayu Tech based in Mumbai, India.

  • The Problem

    Many issues exist around safety, accuracy and cost, for many manual work situations such as picking, packing, stock take and security.

  • Introducing MAiT

    A smart blend of robotics engineering and IoT sensors, MAiT is able to flexibly adapt to various operational scenarios suited for automation with the addition of task-specific sensors, programming, and smart features. Based on a Remote Operated Vehicle (ROV) model, MAiT can be controlled and managed remotely. It can lift objects, detect and record motion, and can even climb steps!

  • Today

    MAiT is being trialled as a prototype for specific supply chain and retail operations in Australia and India. The results of the trial shall be utilised to finalise a market ready product for industrial application.
