Smart City Lighting

  • Smart City Lighting

    ADAPT's Smart Lighting Solution is out to prove that technology can create environmental, economic, and social benefits to humanize cities.

  • The Problem

    Cities grow, and as they do, the infrastructure challenges grow too. Streetlights are an essential part of a city's environment, offering obvious function, as well as providing citizens sense of safety. Unfortunately, most types of outdoor lighting are also a major energy draw.

  • Turn on ADAPT's Smart Lighting

    Developed in house, ADAPT's Smart Lighting Solution is currently undergoing company trials. Retro-fitted to existing and new LED Street Lamps it can provide data on energy usage, and current lamp life. More importantly, the device enables dimming and other control features of the lamp remotely. This ultimately means local councils can not only gain valuable analytics into their city's street lighting, they can save energy and money.

  • Today

    Councils will be able to reduce energy consumption, cost, and maintenance on existing infrastructure without unnecessary expenditure on major physical upgrades. Just another example of the way in which technology applied in the right way can create lasting commercial and social benefit.
