INTERNET OF THINGS (IoT)

The Internet of Things is an idea that has the potential to not just change the way you work, but also the way you live. Basically, IoT is the concept of connecting any device to the internet (or through the internet to each other). IoT postulates a near-future well beyond the interconnectivity you presently experience with smartphones, television and computers, a future where interactivity includes everything from washing machines, coffee makers, headphones, lights, wearable devices, to entire cities. Anything that can be connected, will be connected.

Companies looking seriously at ways to bring IoT to the forefront of their business, position themselves to be in the new wave of innovators, and create fresh opportunities in the expanding digital marketplace.
