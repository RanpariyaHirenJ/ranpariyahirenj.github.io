The year 1991 was a momentous occasion which saw the foundations of the Auto Hangar Group being laid. Thanks to the visionary approach by J. V. Mariwala Group, a leading automobile dealership in Western India. They timely recognized the need to introduce world-class motoring to the Indian car connoisseur and offer him an experience unparalleled.

As the Group evolved to focus more on customer needs, the best minds sat across the table to design an impeccable service framework that would fulfill various requirements. Right from a range of high-end imported cars and Luxury cars the Group also carved a niche for itself by offering impeccable after sales service to its esteemed clientele & customers besides genuine spare parts for the vehicles sold through the dealership.
