Coffee Arabica The original coffee first made in the highlands of Ethiopia

Roasted Arabica Coffee bean is the first variety of coffee that human beings consumed. It was a tree that was indigenous to the southwestern highlands of Ethiopia and was originally named the coffee shrub of Arabia or Arabica Coffee. We source our Arabica Coffee from the coffee- growing regions of Karnataka, Kerala and Tamil Nadu in India. Arabica Coffee is known for its delicate flavour and balanced aroma coupled with a sharp/sweet taste. Arabica Coffees normally have half the caffeine content of Robusta Coffee.
