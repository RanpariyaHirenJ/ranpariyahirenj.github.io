How fast is fast? Depends on the car.

Share

Fastned provided a very interesting comparison of DC fast charging curves of several popular electric car models, including the BMW i3, Hyundai IONIQ Electric, Nissan LEAF, Opel Ampera-e and Volkswagen e-Golf.

Auto manufacturers are committing to fewer gas-powered vehicles, so we see the biggest development is the overall 'pull' being initiated by those car makers for infrastructure that'll help support that enormous growth in vehicles over the coming decades.

Convenience and "range confidence" will grow enormously, with dramatic drops in "fueling" time as Level 2 and DC Fast charging come online.
