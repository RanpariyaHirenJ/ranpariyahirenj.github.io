2018 Kia Soul Exterior Paint Color and Interior Fabric Options

Have you ever wanted to deck out a Kia Soul crossover in an orange paint color? We do, too – it reminds us of happy times, warmth, health, and overall good moods. So we have great news for you – the 2018 Kia Soul, which began arriving on lots like ours in the summer of 2017, has a new Wild Orange exterior paint color!

We will break down the exterior paint colors and interior fabrics you can choose for the 2018 Kia Soul trim-by-trim, after the page break!

Not only is there a new color option for 2018 in Wild Orange, there's plenty of others to feast your eyes on, from the bold Inferno Red to the audacious Alien 2, among others.

All told, there are 11 exterior color options for this improved model. We have the trim-by-trim list, below.

The BMW i3 was offered in two battery versions - of around 22 and 33 kWh. The higher capacity cells/pack allow it to charge a little quicker (peak of almost 50 kW) and for an extended state-of-charge frame:

  • Base trim (4 total): Clear White, Bright Silver, Alien 2, Shadow Black.
  • Plus (+) trim (11 total): Clear White, White / Red, Bright Silver, Titanium Gray, Alien 2, Wild Orange, Inferno Red, Inferno Red / Black, Caribbean Blue, Mysterious Blue, Shadow Black.
  • Exclaim (!) trim (5 total): Clear White, Bright Silver, Titanium Gray, Inferno Red, Shadow Black.

Interior Fabric Options: 2018 Soul

  • Base trim: Black Tricot Cloth.
  • Plus (+) trim: Gray Two-tone Leather, Black Woven Cloth, Designer Collection Black Woven Cloth, Gray Two-Tone Woven Cloth.
  • Exclaim (!) trim: Black Leather.

Benefits of the 2018 Kia Soul

  • Three engine options, with 130, 161 and 201 HP.
  • MPG: 25 city / 30 highway base, and up to 26 city, 31 highway.
  • Ample cargo room in back, with up to 61.3 cubic feet.
  • Versatile, with sedan-like handling and SUV-like cargo room.
  • Spacious interior, with 101 cubic feet for passengers with no sunroof.

2018 Kia Soul: New Features

To go along with what we've outlined above, here's some upgrades and new features you can find in the fun, iconic, and versatile 2018 Kia Soul!

  • New Wild Orange exterior paint color for the Plus trim.
  • UVO available for Base trim, standard in both Plus and Exclaim trims (was available for Plus, standard in Exclaim trim in 2017 model year).
  • New driver-assist safety features available for the Plus trim: Smart Cruise Control, Autonomous Emergency Braking.
  • High Intensity Discharge (HID) headlights, power-folding outside mirrors available for the Plus trim (for the 2017 model both were only available in the Exclaim trim)
  • Mirror-mounted LED turn signal indicators also newly-available for the Plus trim.
