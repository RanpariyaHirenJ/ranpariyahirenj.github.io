VR-Magic Experience the magic of virtual reality

What is VR-Magic?

VR-Magic is a web application that uses the latest technology of 360 panorama photography and delivers on your mobile handset screen/ computer screen, an immersive virtual walkthrough.

Who is it applicable for and how?

If you are an architect, a real estate developer, interior designer, an experience centre, or just another person having a beautifully designed piece of property that you wish to display to prospective buyers, VR-Magic is the solution for you.

  • Helps you present to your customers a whole new experience of observing your infrastructure, whether it's a hotel, showroom, villa or apartment. It can be viewed on the Internet in a 360 degree panoramic dimension.

  • Use of hotspots and navigation waypoints gives your audience an opportunity to navigate across your property and to view the information related to key items of interest on a click.

  • Additional incorporation of wish list and shopping cart can bring in a completely new factor of virtual shopping. In short, it's a radical innovation in the space of virtual reality.
