VR-Pro Immersive virtual reality video walkthrough mobile app

VR-Pro!

VR-Pro is an immersive 360 (Panoramic) videos based mobile app for video walkthroughs that is compatible with VR head gears, and is operated using interactive voice commands. With VR-Pro, one can experience 360 videos with the perception of being physically present at the site of action.

It has been designed keeping in mind the latest virtual reality head gears such as Samsung VR, Google Cardboard and the Oculus Rift. When you are in VR mode, interactivity is performed using motion and voice commands.

Who is it for?

VR-Pro has been designed and developed to give a life like experience to a 360 panoramic video. It is applicable to the following verticals in the specified ways:

  • Architects, Interior Designers or Real Estate Developers can showcase 3D animated videos or live 360 video shoots of their sample or popular projects.

  • Factories and industrial setups can provide an interactive guided tour of their factory to their clients or technicians.

  • Hotels can provide a guided tour of their property.

  • Tour operators can provide a guided tour of the holiday destination and the tour itinerary.

  • Event Organizers can provide a 360 virtual experience of their event "LIVE" to their remote viewers, imparting the viewers an experience closest to reality.

VR-Pro imparts to your customer, a whole new level of experiencing your content in virtual reality. With the VR head gear your customers get the experience of being physically present at the site.

Key frames and hotspots along with synthesized voice provide a guided tour experience.

Additional incorporation of voice commands and motion sensors provide a new level of interactivity. The wish list feature allows you to identify your customers' preference during the walkthrough.
