    Jai Shri Krishna A Live Drama ™

    Presented on the stage (indoor and outdoor), in Hindi Language, that would unfold the Life of Lord Shri Krishna from his birth to his sermon to Arjuna, known as Bhagwad Gita.

  • The Show

    The Show

    The Show would have support of popular songs & bhajans, by saints, poets and eminent lyricists along with dances and choreography thereby making it glamorous and quite entertaining. However the beauty or USP of the show would be the two characters, one playing Lord Krishna himself and the other playing Narrator Krishna.

    In the show the `Lord Krishna' would act, sing and dance as Krishna himself. The `Narrator Krishna' would speak in the language of the day to initiate and establish a dialogue with the audiences. He would interpret various incidences in the life of Krishna objectively, and would comment on various texts like the Mahabharata, Puranas, Bhagwat, Harivijay & Bhagwad Gita, critically.

    As the `Lord Krishna' would philosophically enlighten Arjuna on the battleground, the `Narrator Krishna' would enlighten the audiences as to how they should understand Krishna's philosophy and apply it in their day to day life, as they face their problems, fight their wars and struggle to succeed in their battle of life.

    The show is a fine fusion of entertainment and enlightenment. It would have absolute grandeur in its sets, costumes, lights, background music and would have a big LED screen to project scenic backgrounds and attractive images.
