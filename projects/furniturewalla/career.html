Work With Us

FW Furniturewalla is one of India's premium leading brands for luxury furniture, accessories, art & lighting. The brand is associated with Luxe Living and caters to a sophisticated and discerning clientele from around the country. Having furnished lakhs of homes in India , Furniturewalla commands great customer loyalty, trust & satisfaction. FW fosters a highly creative and learning approach among team members with valuable exposure to product sourcing, international procurement, supply chain management, large format retailing, sales & marketing and client relationship management. Led by Laila & Farhan Furniturewalla, who bring in vast and distinguished family experience in furniture and art and fuse it relevantly with glamour and lifestyle trends, FW is an exciting brand to work for. If you are looking to further your career in such an atmosphere, especially in the areas of sales, merchandizing, styling and retailing, do mail your resume to careers@furniturewalla.com.

FW Furniturewalla currently retails from 4 large, signature stores in Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru & Hyderabad and expanding to 4 more centres in India.
